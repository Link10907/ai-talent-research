# TLDR: CNBC — GSK $300M Deal with 23andMe (2018)

**Source:** cnbc.com/2018/07/24/glaxosmithkline-23andme-team-up-on-genetics-driven-drug-research.html  
**Date:** 2018-07-24  
**Type:** article

## Key Facts
- GlaxoSmithKline invested $300M in 23andMe for equity stake
- 4-year exclusive collaboration for drug target discovery
- Partnership announced July 25, 2018
- At time: 23andMe had 5M customers; 80% opted into research program
- Equal split of funding and IP from resulting therapeutics
- GSK extended for 5th year in 2023 via $50M payment
- Result: 40+ therapeutic programs; CD96 antibody into clinical trial

## Relevance
Primary source on 23andMe's B2B monetization model (private data → pharma deal)
