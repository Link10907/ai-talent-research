# TLDR: SEC LinkedIn Form 8-K FY2011 — Revenue Breakdown

**Source:** sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0001271024/000119312511295106/d251469dex991.htm  
**Date:** 2011 Q3/Q4 earnings  
**Type:** s1_filing (SEC Form 8-K earnings release)

## Key Facts
- Q3 2011: Hiring Solutions revenue = $71.0M = 51% of total revenue
- Q4 2011: Hiring Solutions revenue = $84.9M = 50% of total revenue
- Q1 2012: Hiring Solutions = $102.6M = 54% of total revenue
- Q3 2012: Talent Solutions = $138.4M = 55% of total revenue
- LinkedIn IPO: May 19, 2011 on NYSE
- Hiring Solutions renamed Talent Solutions in 2012

## Relevance
Primary SEC source confirming LinkedIn Recruiter/Hiring Solutions was the dominant revenue line from IPO day; the B-side data model was the core business from inception
