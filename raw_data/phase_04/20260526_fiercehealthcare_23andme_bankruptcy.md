# TLDR: Fierce Healthcare — 23andMe Bankruptcy Data Concerns (2025)

**Date:** 2025-03  
**Type:** article

## Key Facts
- 23andMe filed Chapter 11 bankruptcy March 23, 2025 in Delaware
- 15M+ customers' genetic data at stake
- ~80% opted into research program (de-identified data sharing)
- 2023 data breach: 6.9M records exposed (credential stuffing)
- Sale: TTAM Research Institute (Wojcicki nonprofit) won auction at $305M
- Sale closed July 14, 2025
- 28 AGs coalition argued against data transfer without renewed consent
- Congressional investigation launched April 2025
