# Mem.ai — AI Notes, Stalled Trajectory

## Source date range: 2022–2025

## Growth Curve
- 2022: raised $23.5M from OpenAI Startup Fund; post-money valuation $110M
- Product: AI-powered note-taking with automatic organization ("Mem X" feature)
- No official user count ever published
- 2023–2024: negative Reddit/community feedback on bugs, UX issues, missing features
- 2024: still operational (mem.ai live), but limited new product announcements
- Oct 2025: updownradar.com tracking login issues → site experiencing instability
- Status as of 2025: operational but stagnant; no pivot announcement found in public record

## Pricing (2024–2025)
- Free tier (limited)
- Mem Starter: ~$8.33/month
- Mem Pro: ~$14.99/month (AI features)
- Teams: custom

## Key Failure Points (from community reporting)
- "Incredibly buggy, insecure, missing in features, flawed in design" (Medium analysis)
- Competition from Notion AI (2023), Apple Notes AI improvements, Google integration
- Steep learning curve; no clear workflow for most users
- OpenAI fund investment created pressure to show AI-first differentiation that didn't materialize at scale

## Note: Mem0 (separate company)
- Mem0 (formerly Embedchain) raised $24M (YC + others, Oct 2025) for "memory layer for AI apps"
- This is NOT the same as Mem.ai; different company, different product
