# Instagram — Default Public Social Network

## Source date range: 2024–2025

## Scale
- 3B monthly active users (MAU) — 2024
- 500M daily active users (DAU) — DAU/MAU ratio = ~16.7% (lower than LinkedIn)
- Owned by Meta; revenue not broken out separately

## % Users Public
- Default = public account for new users
- No official 2024 stat on public vs private accounts
- Historical estimate (2016): ~300M private accounts out of then-500M total = ~60% public
- Current: Instagram defaults to public; private account requires manual toggle
- Brands: virtually all public; personal accounts skew more private, especially younger users

## Retention
- Strong: Instagram Stories retention above 75% for up to 7 frames (2024 benchmark)
- App-level DAU/MAU = 16.7% (lower than LinkedIn; Instagram used more for browsing than daily posting)
- Long-term: Meta reports Instagram as one of its strongest retention apps

## Monetization Path
- Advertising: Meta's primary revenue driver
- Instagram alone estimated $32B+ in ad revenue (2024)
- No B2B data product in the Recruiter/LinkedIn sense
- Creator economy: subscriptions, badges, paid partnerships — secondary

## Design Pattern
- Public feed creates content loop; private DMs create intimacy layer
- Stories = semi-ephemeral semi-public escape valve
- Close Friends = private sub-layer within public app

## Fit with "Feed B-Side Data" Goal
- LOW fit for our specific use case (AI talent)
- High-fit for advertising model; not applicable to B2B professional intelligence
