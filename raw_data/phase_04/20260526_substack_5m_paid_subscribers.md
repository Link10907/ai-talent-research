# TLDR: Substack — 5 Million Paid Subscribers Milestone (March 2025)

**Source:** tubefilter.com/2025/03/12/substack-five-million-paid-subscribers-journalist-reporter-newsletter  
**Date:** March 2025  
**Type:** article

## Key Facts
- 5M paid subscriptions announced March 2025
- 50M total active subscriptions (paid + free) — 2026
- ~100K publications earning money (April 2026); was 50K in May 2025
- Growth: 1M paid (2021) → 3M (2024) → 5M (March 2025)
- Platform takes 10% of paid subscription revenue
- No advertising; no data sales
- Substack Notes (Twitter-like): public by default, launched 2023
- All newsletters/posts: public by default unless explicitly paywalled

## Relevance
Public-default publishing platform; demonstrates that public content does not automatically create B2B data value — direct creator monetization is the model instead
