- Note: Business of Apps $163M vs Latka $500M — significant discrepancy; use $163M as conservative, $500M as aggressive estimate
- Revenue model: 90% from premium subscriptions
- Premium subscription: $79.99/year or $11.99/month; Family: $139.99/year
- Valuation: $2.2B
- Activities: 4B published in 2025
- Kudos: 14B given in 2025 (up 2B YoY)
