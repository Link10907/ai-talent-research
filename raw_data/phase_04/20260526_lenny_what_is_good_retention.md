# TLDR: Lenny Rachitsky — What Is Good Retention (Benchmarks)

**Source:** lennysnewsletter.com/p/what-is-good-retention-issue-29  
**Date:** Originally 2021; referenced as current benchmark through 2024  
**Type:** substack_post

## Key Facts (from Lenny + Casey Winters framework, survey of 20 growth practitioners)

### 6-Month Retention Benchmarks:
- Consumer Social (Twitter, Facebook): Good = 25%; Great = 45%
- Consumer Transactional (Airbnb, Lyft): Good = 30%; Great = 50%
- Consumer SaaS (Netflix, Spotify): Good = 40%; Great = 70%

### D30 Context:
- Industry median D30: ~7% (mobile apps)
- SaaS average D30: 20–35%
- D30 = clearest signal of product-market fit

### D90:
- Measures long-term loyalty; users still active = core base
- Benchmarks vary significantly by category

## Relevance
The standard retention benchmark framework for evaluating all products in Phase 04; all "unknown" retention entries should be compared against these benchmarks when estimates become available
