## TLDR
HN thread discussing Read.cv's acquisition by Perplexity (announced January 17, 2025). Sentiment: predominantly negative/disappointed. Core user complaint: "Users are increasingly treated like stepping stools for founders...users help grow a product...get comfortable with that product, integrate it into their lives...and then it's yanked out from under them" (JadoJodo). Another user (pixeldrifter) called it "a betrayal" — focus on "cashing out" vs. building for community. tobr noted the shutdown schedule was "aggressive" but acknowledged users could self-host via Next.js download. dorian-graph: "Yet again, let down by a centralised platform" — recommended decade-long forums over startup services. Community recommended alternatives: Reactive Resume (open-source self-hosted), twigg.social, tini.bio. Andy Chung (founder) called the announcement "incredibly bittersweet" and end of "our time with @read_cv."
