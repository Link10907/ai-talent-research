## TLDR
Polywork's Series B: $28M, led by Caffeinated Capital, announced September 15, 2022 — simultaneous with public beta launch. This was Polywork's largest round and came after prior funding of approximately $13–16.5M (seed + Series A). Total raised by Series B: approximately $41.5–44.5M. The "public beta launch" timing reveals Polywork was invite-only from 2021 through September 2022 — 18 months of private beta. This is significant: the invite-only buzz drove valuation without actual product-market proof at scale. The Series B was supposed to fund growth from private beta into mass market — that growth never materialized, leading to the January 2025 shutdown.
