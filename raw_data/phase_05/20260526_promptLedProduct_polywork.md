## TLDR
Post-mortem analysis of Polywork. Founded 2021 by Peter Johnston as "LinkedIn alternative showing your whole professional identity." Raised $13M total with backers including Stripe Collison brothers, Alexis Ohanian, Elad Gil. Won Product Hunt Golden Kitty Award 2022. Closed January 31, 2025. Identified failure factors: (1) underestimated LinkedIn's network effect; (2) cold-start problem never solved — professional networks need critical mass; (3) launched during "tech fatigue" — poor adoption timing; (4) $13M funding created growth pressure; (5) late 2023 pivot to website builder confused early community without solving core problems. Key lesson: ruthlessly validate market need, ensure crystal-clear differentiation, plan for long runway. Note: this source cites $13M total raised; BusinessWire confirms $28M Series B alone in Sep 2022, suggesting total was likely $41.5M or $44.5M as cited in the brief.
