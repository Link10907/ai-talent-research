## TLDR
TechCrunch breaking news on Read.cv acquisition. Founded 2021 by Andy Chung (former product designer at Facebook, Mozilla, Quip). Platform positioned as "show don't tell professional network" — portfolio sharing, professional identity, team profiles, job listings, candidate search. Team size: ~3 employees at acquisition. Recent product: "Sites" (personal websites with .cv domains). Backers: F7 Ventures, Fanjul Capital (amounts undisclosed). User count at acquisition: not disclosed. Perplexity rationale: talent — CEO Aravind Srinivas praised team as "incredibly capable in designing and building consumer and social experiences." Perplexity expanding into enterprise/corporate features. Third Perplexity acquisition (after Carbon and Spellwise). Platform began winding down January 17, 2025; users had until May 16, 2025 to export data.
