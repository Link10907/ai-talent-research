## TLDR
At peak (September 2020), Lunchclub was a 15-person startup valued at $100M+ after $24M Series A from Coatue and Lightspeed. Founded November 2017 by Vladimir Novakovski, Scott Wu, Hayley Leibson. AI-powered 1-on-1 video meeting matching. COVID added video chat in March 2020; usage spiked dramatically. Series A led by Coatue and Lightspeed Venture Partners. Prior: $4M seed from a16z. Total raised: ~$28M. The COVID usage spike was the product's peak moment — never disclosed specific MAU/DAU numbers. Post-COVID fade was gradual and undramatic.
