# TLDR: ChatGPT / OpenAI Paid Subscriber Metrics

**Date collected**: 2025-05-26
**Source type**: The Information (shareholder disclosure), Backlinko, Business of Apps

## Key Numbers
- Paid subscribers end of 2024: 15.5M (nearly 3× from 5.8M in 2023)
- All-tier subscribers H2 2025: 50M+
- ChatGPT Plus: $20/month
- ChatGPT Pro: $200/month (launched late 2024); halved to $100/month April 2026
- Weekly active users (2026 estimate): 900M
- Implied conversion (2024): 15.5M / ~300M registered ≈ 5%

## Context
- 80% of growth 2023→2024 was organic (no paid acquisition)
- Enterprise + Teams = significant portion of paid revenue
- Conversion is driven by power users hitting free tier limits (GPT-4 caps)

## Comparison: Claude
- Anthropic does not disclose subscriber counts
- Claude Pro: $20/month; Max: $200/month
- ~80% of Anthropic revenue from API/enterprise (not consumer)

## Why This Matters
5% consumer conversion for a general-purpose AI tool is the floor expectation. Cursor at 36% = ceiling for highly specialized, immediately-ROI tools. Professional tools targeting specific use cases should target 10–20% conversion.

## Confidence
High for ChatGPT figures (The Information sourced shareholder disclosures). Low for Claude (Anthropic does not disclose).
