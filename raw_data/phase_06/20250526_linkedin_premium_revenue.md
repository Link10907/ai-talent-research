# TLDR: LinkedIn Premium Revenue & Subscriber Data

**Date collected**: 2025-05-26
**Source type**: LinkedIn official press release (March 2024), TechCrunch (Jan 2025), Microsoft earnings

## Key Numbers
- FY2024 Premium revenue: $1.7B
- FY2025 Premium revenue: $2B+ (first time crossing $2B)
- Total LinkedIn revenue FY2025: $17.81B
- Premium as % of total: ~12.5%
- Premium sign-up growth: +51% in FY2024
- 40% of Premium subscribers using new AI features
- Estimated Premium subscribers: 175M+ (analyst estimates — LinkedIn does not officially disclose)

## Pricing Reference
- Career: $39.99/month
- Business: $59.99/month
- Sales Navigator Core: $99.99/seat/month

## ARPU Estimate
$2B revenue / 175M subscribers = ~$11.40/month implied — but this mixes Career, Business, Sales Nav
Actual ARPU probably $15–25/month weighted average

## Why This Matters
LinkedIn Premium at $2B ARR is largest professional subscription business in the world by revenue. Proves professionals will pay $40–$100/month for career/sales tools when ROI is quantifiable.

## Confidence
Revenue: High (official LinkedIn/Microsoft disclosures). Subscriber count: Medium (analyst estimate; not officially disclosed).
