# TLDR: Substack Platform Conversion & Revenue

**Date collected**: 2025-05-26
**Source type**: Sacra analysis, Backlinko aggregation, independent newsletter analyses

## Key Numbers
- Active subscriptions: 50M total (2025)
- Paid subscriptions: 5M (March 2025)
- Platform paid rate: ~10% of active subscriptions
- Gross writer revenue: $370M (2024), $450M (2025)
- Substack take (10%): $37M (2024), $45M (2025)
- Median per-publication conversion: 3%
- Tech niche conversion: 8% (highest category)
- Writers earning any money: 100,000 publications

## Why This Matters
Real median is 3%, not Substack's claimed 5–10%. This recalibrates expectations. Niche = better conversion. Professional/tech audience pays at 2–3× the general population rate.

## Confidence
High for platform-level; Medium for per-publication median.
