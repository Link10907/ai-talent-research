# TLDR: LinkedIn OpenToWork Signal Data

**Date collected**: 2025-05-26
**Source type**: CNBC (Jan 2025), Yahoo Finance, DemandSage

## Key Numbers
- Total "open to work" (public + private): 220M+ (35% YoY increase)
- Public #OpenToWork badge: 28–40M (3–4% of LinkedIn members)
- Private/recruiter-only signal: ~180–200M (~22% of members)
- Badge doubles recruiter contact rate
- Feature launched: 2020

## The Critical 5:1 Ratio
For every 1 person showing the public badge, ~5 are privately signaling. The social cost of "looking desperate" suppresses visibility 5×.

## Why This Matters for AI Talent Product
220M people are actively or passively open to work at any given time on LinkedIn alone. Any product that reduces the social cost of signaling captures this latent demand. The visibility problem is not supply (candidates willing to be found) — it's friction and stigma.

## Confidence
High. LinkedIn data cited via CNBC; pattern confirmed across multiple sources.
