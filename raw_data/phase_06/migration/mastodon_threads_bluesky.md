# Mastodon / Threads / Bluesky — Migration Data from X/Twitter

## Mastodon
### User Growth (2022 Twitter Migration)
| Date | Registered Accounts | Monthly Active Users |
|------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Jan 2022 | 2.7M | 294,000 |
| Nov 2022 (Musk acquisition peak) | 2.6M spike in weeks | 1.8M MAU end of year |
| Dec 2022 | 5.8M registered | 2.0M MAU |
| March 2023 | 10M+ registered | ~1.8M MAU |
| 2024+ | ~10.5M registered | 750K–1M MAU |

### Key Insight
- 75,000 new users/day at peak migration surge (Nov 2022)
- Massive gap: 10.5M registered vs ~1M active = **90% abandonment rate**
- Curiosity converts; daily habit does not form

## Bluesky
### Growth Timeline
| Date | Registered Users |
|------|----------------|
| Sept 2024 | ~10M |
| Oct 2024 | 13M |
| Nov 2024 (election spike) | 20M+ (13M added in 6 weeks) |
| Jan 2025 | 27.4M (+174% in 4 months) |
| Nov 2025 | 40.2M |

### Engagement Decline Post-Peak
- Peak single-day likes: 2,789,693 (Nov 18, 2024)
- June 2025 single-day likes: 998,390 (−64% from peak)
- Clear pattern: event-driven spikes, retention falls significantly

## Comparative Summary
| Platform | Peak Spike Cause | Retained? |
|----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Mastodon | Musk acquisition Nov 2022 | ~10% of registered accounts |
| Threads | Instagram integration July 2023 | ~25% DAU/MAU after 18 months |
| Bluesky | US election Nov 2024 | Engagement −64% post-peak |

## Key Signal
Migration from X is real but ephemeral. Users "try" alternatives but return to X or disperse. No alternative has achieved Twitter-level daily habit. Bluesky closest demographically (18–34, tech-leaning).
