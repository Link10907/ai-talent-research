# Bento.me — Decline & Shutdown

## Timeline
- **Founded**: 2022 by Sélim Benayat and Mugeeb Hassan
- **Position**: Link-in-bio tool; rich, beautiful mini landing pages
- **Acquisition**: Acquired by Linktree (date not specified in sources)
- **Shutdown date**: February 13, 2026
- **Post-shutdown**: All user data deleted; website unavailable

## User Data
- **User count**: Never publicly disclosed
- **Retention**: Described as declining; product appeared "discontinued/no longer updated" before official shutdown
- **Product Hunt presence**: Listed and upvoted; community appreciated aesthetic

## Why It Declined
- Linktree acquisition led to product neglect
- Competed against well-funded Linktree, Carrd, Webflow, Later (link-in-bio)
- Market commoditized; free tiers eroded monetization
- No strong professional differentiation to retain creators

## Migration
- Users migrated to Linktree, Carrd, own.page, personal sites
- No dramatic exodus narrative — gradual abandonment

## Key Signal for Our Product
- Link-in-bio as standalone product insufficient — needs platform integration
- Creator tools need ongoing development signal to retain users
- Being acquired often signals wind-down risk → user trust erodes

## Evidence Quality
- Shutdown: **High** (taplink.at, own.page coverage)
- User count: **Unknown**
- Reason: **Low** (inferred from circumstances)
