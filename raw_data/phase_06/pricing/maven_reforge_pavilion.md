# Maven / Reforge / Pavilion — Pricing & Revenue Data

## Maven (Cohort-Based Courses Platform)
- **Completion rate**: ~75% (May 2021 internal data); cohort-based format generally 85–96% vs 5–15% self-paced
- **Course pricing**: Typically $500–$2,000/course (instructor-set)
- **20,000 students** as of early 2025 (largest live learning platform claim)
- **2023**: 15,000 course completions (Maven Analytics year-in-review)
- No public ARR or revenue disclosed
- Evidence quality: **Medium** (founder posts, 2021 internal data)

## Reforge (Professional Upskilling, B2B Focus)
- **Individual membership**: $1,995/year (as of Dec 2024 restructure)
- **Teams Starter**: $9,995/year for 10 seats
- **Teams Scale**: $23,995/year for 30 seats
- **Members**: 50,000+ professionals
- **Growth**: 15% subscriber growth by Q1 2025; platform usage up 30% in 2024
- **Revenue mix**: 90% from subscriptions in 2024
- **Funding**: $81M raised (Command.ai analysis)
- Previously: cohort-based courses at ~$2,000/course before membership pivot

## Pavilion (GTM Leaders Community)
- **Members**: 10,000+
- **Pricing**: Not publicly disclosed (application-based)
- **2023 LinkedIn organic revenue add**: $3M ARR
- **Profile**: VP+ GTM/Sales/Marketing operators; invite-only tiers
- Evidence quality: **Low** (pricing not public; member count from website)
