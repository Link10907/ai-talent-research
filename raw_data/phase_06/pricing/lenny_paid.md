# Lenny's Newsletter — Paid Subscriber Data

## Key Numbers (Verified)
- **Total subscribers**: 1,000,000 (milestone announced March 4, 2025)
- **Paid subscribers (2024 est.)**: ~18,000–25,000
  - As of April 2023: 18,000 paid / 377,000 free = **4–5% conversion rate**
  - After 1M milestone (March 2025), paid likely higher — unconfirmed
- **Pricing**: $15/month or ~$150/year (annual plan)
- **Annual revenue from subscriptions**: ~$2M+ (estimated from paid × price)
- **Podcast revenue**: $500,000+/year (CNBC, Jan 2024)
