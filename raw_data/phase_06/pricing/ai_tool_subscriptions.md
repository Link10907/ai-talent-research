# AI Tool Paid Subscriptions — Data Summary

## ChatGPT / OpenAI
- **Paid subscribers (end 2024)**: 15.5M (nearly tripled from 5.8M in 2023; The Information)
- **All tiers (2025)**: 50M+ paying subscribers (COO disclosed)
- **Free users**: 900M weekly active (2026 estimate)
- **Implied conversion**: 15.5M / ~300M registered = ~5% paid conversion (2024)
- **ChatGPT Plus price**: $20/month
- **ChatGPT Pro**: $200/month (launched late 2024; halved to $100 in April 2026)
- **ChatGPT Team**: ~$30/seat/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom

## Claude (Anthropic)
- **Claude Pro**: $20/month; $200/month for Max
- **Paid subscriber trend**: Doubled in early 2026 (record growth)
- **Revenue split**: ~80% from business/API; ~20% consumer
- **No official subscriber count disclosed**
- **Estimate (2025)**: 18–30M total users; paid fraction unknown

## Cursor (AI Code Editor)
- **Paid users (early 2025)**: 360,000+
- **ARR trajectory**: $4M → $48M (spring to Oct 2024); $400M (late 2024); $1B (early 2025)
- **Freemium conversion rate**: **36%** (vs. industry average 2–5%) — exceptional
- **Pricing**: $20/month (Pro), $40/month (Business)
- **Customer count (Aug 2024)**: 40,000+

## Perplexity Pro
- Pricing: $20/month or $200/year
- No public subscriber count

## Key Benchmark: AI Tool Willingness to Pay
- Premium AI tools price at $20/month (consumer) to $40+/month (professional)
- ChatGPT Plus conversion ~5% of registered users in 2024
- Cursor anomaly: 36% conversion — driven by immediate productivity proof point

## Evidence Quality
- ChatGPT subscriber numbers: **High** (shareholder disclosures via The Information)
- Claude subscriber count: **Low** (Anthropic does not disclose)
- Cursor data: **High** (widely reported from funding disclosures)
