# Substack Paid Conversion Rate Data

## Platform-Level Numbers (2024–2025)
- **Total active subscriptions**: 50 million (2025); 35 million (Sept 2025)
- **Paid subscriptions**: 5 million (March 2025)
- **Platform-wide paid conversion**: ~10% of active subscriptions = paid
- **Writers earning money**: 100,000 publications (April 2026); ~17,000 with recurring paid subs
- **Gross writer revenue**: $370M (2024), $450M (2025)
- **Substack's revenue (10% take)**: ~$37M (2024), ~$45M (2025)

## Per-Publication Conversion Rates
- **Substack's own claim**: 5–10% paid conversion is "normal"
- **Actual median (real data)**: ~3% (analysis of live publications)
- **Top 20% of publications**: >5% conversion
- **Tech niche**: 8% conversion rate (highest category)
- **General/large audience publications**: 1–2%
- **Specialized/niche publishers**: 4–10%

## Key Insight
Substack's 5–10% benchmark is aspirational. Real median is 3%. Niche professional content (like PM, tech, finance) dramatically outperforms general content. Lenny at 4–5% is therefore mid-range for his niche.

## Evidence Quality
- Platform totals: **High** (Substack/Sacra published figures)
- Per-publication conversion: **High** (independent analysis of live data)
- Individual publication benchmarks: **Medium**
