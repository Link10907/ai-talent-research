# Notion — Pricing & User Data

## Current Pricing (2024–2025)
- **Free**: Limited blocks, 10MB file upload
- **Plus** (formerly Personal Pro): $10/month ($8/mo annual)
- **Business**: $15/seat/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom
- **AI add-on**: $8/seat/month (added 2023)

## Historical Context
- Personal Pro was $5/month when launched (before 2023 restructure)
- Removed free Personal Pro → caused user backlash 2023

## Financial Data
- Notion is **private**; no public revenue or ARPU figures
- Valuation: $10B (Oct 2021 Series C, $275M raised)
- Revenue estimate (2023): ~$100M ARR (various analyst estimates)
- No confirmed 2024 revenue or paid user count

## Comparable Data Points
- Obsidian Sync: $8/month (no free tier for sync)
- Bear (notes app): $2.99/month
- Notion competes on collaboration vs. individual note tools

## Evidence Quality
- Pricing: **High** (publicly listed)
- Revenue/users: **Low** (private company, unconfirmed estimates)
- Valuation: **High** (confirmed from funding round)

## Key Insight for Model
Professional knowledge tools price at $8–$15/seat/month. Individual personal tools price lower ($3–$8/month). Enterprise deals 3–5x individual pricing.
