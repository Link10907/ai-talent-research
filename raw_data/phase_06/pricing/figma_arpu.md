# Figma — ARPU and Revenue Data

## Revenue (2024–2025)
- **2024 revenue**: $749M (up 48% YoY from $505M in 2023)
- **Q1 2025 revenue**: $228M (46% YoY growth → $912M ARR run-rate)
- **S-1 filed**: 2025; IPO pending
- **Rule of 40 score**: 63 (Q1 2025) — well above 40 benchmark

## User Metrics
- **Monthly Active Users**: 13M+
- **Customers (orgs)**: 1B+ (as of 2025)
- **Net Dollar Retention**: 134% (2024); 132% (Q1 2025)
- **Developer users**: ~30% of MAU; now monetized via Dev Seats

## Pricing Tiers
- **Free**: Basic features
- **Starter** (formerly Professional): ~$12/editor/month
- **Organization**: $45/editor/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing
- **FigJam**: separate pricing tier

## Revenue Mix
- **Organization + Enterprise**: 70% of total revenue
- Bottom-up PLG model: individuals join free, orgs upgrade

## Implied ARPU
- $749M revenue / 13M MAU = ~$57.6 ARPU per MAU (2024)
- But paid editor seats are fraction of MAU; actual per-paying-seat ARPU much higher
- Estimated paid seats: 2–4M → implied ~$190–$375/seat/year

## Evidence Quality
- Revenue figures: **High** (S-1 filing level)
- ARPU calculation: **Medium** (derived from public numbers)
- Seat count: **Low** (estimated)
