# LinkedIn Premium — Paid User & Revenue Data

## Revenue Milestones
- **FY2024 Premium revenue**: $1.7B (announced March 2024 by LinkedIn)
- **FY2025 Premium revenue**: $2B+ (milestone reported Jan 2025, TechCrunch)
- **FY2025 total LinkedIn revenue**: $17.81B (9% YoY growth)
- **Premium as % of total revenue**: ~12.5%

## Subscriber Estimates
- **Estimated Premium subscribers**: 175M+ (various analyst estimates for 2024)
- **Premium sign-up growth**: +51% in FY2024 (LinkedIn official)
- **Premium + AI adoption**: 40% of Premium subscribers using AI features

## Pricing Tiers (2024)
| Tier | Monthly Price |
|------|--------------|
| Premium Career | $39.99/month |
| Premium Business | $59.99/month |
| Sales Navigator Core | $99.99/seat/month |
| Sales Navigator Advanced | $169.99/seat/month |
| Recruiter Lite | $170/month |
| Recruiter | Enterprise pricing |

## Key Insight
- $2B revenue / 175M subscribers = ~$11.40 ARPU/month if all subscribers equal — but tiers vary 4×
- Premium Career + Business subs much more numerous; Sales Navigator fewer but higher value
- LinkedIn Premium is primarily professional career/sales tooling — not social

## Evidence Quality
- Revenue: **High** (LinkedIn / Microsoft earnings)
- Subscriber count: **Medium** (analyst estimates; LinkedIn does not disclose count)
- Pricing: **High** (public)
