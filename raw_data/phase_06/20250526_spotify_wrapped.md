## The Psychology
Spotify collects data passively (users don't think about it). Wrapped converts surveillance into gift. The insight is identity-confirming ("Your Listening Age is 1990s") and socially shareable. Users don't experience Wrapped as "my data was used" — they experience it as "Spotify made something cool about me."

## The Formula
Data collected passively + Repackaged as personalized identity mirror + Shared publicly as social signal = Viral engagement with zero data consent friction
