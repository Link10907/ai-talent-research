# Hampton & South Park Commons — Premium Community Retention

## Hampton
### Overview
- Founded by Sam Parr (The Hustle founder)
- Target: Tech founders with $3M+ revenue or $10M+ exit
- Model: Peer accountability groups of 5–8 people meeting monthly

### Metrics (2024–2025)
- **Members**: 1,000+ (as of July 2024 and Jan 2026)
- **ARR**: $8M (Jan 2026)
- **Annual membership fee**: $8,500/year
- **Core groups**: 95+ groups of 5–8 members; 10–20 new groups added per quarter
- **Revenue/member requirements**: $3M ARR (raised from $1M in Aug 2024) or $10M past exit
- **Average member revenue**: ~$23M/year

### Revenue Math
- 1,000 members × $8,500 = $8.5M — consistent with $8M ARR (accounting for some churn)
- **Implied annual renewal rate**: Very high given the member profile and peer-group stickiness

### Retention Mechanics
- Small peer groups (5–8) create social accountability
- Losing access = losing peer relationships (high switching cost)
- Exclusive community signal (net worth/revenue gate)

## South Park Commons (SPC)
### Overview
- San Francisco-based community for people "in transition" — between jobs, exploring ideas
- Model: 6-month exploratory residency; fund-backed (no member fees)
- Not a paid community — funded by the SPC fund (7% equity / SAFE investment model)

### Metrics
- **Total members (all time)**: 1,000+ (over past decade)
- **2024**: Opened first international location (Bengaluru, India with Flipkart's Binny Bansal)
- **Member intent split**: 70% start companies; 30% pursue research/open-source
- **Membership duration**: 6 months (structured residency)
- **Cost to members**: Free (supported by fund)

### Retention Mechanics
- Fund investment creates incentive to stay engaged
- Small cohort sizes → deep relationships
- SPC alumni network is the long-term retention mechanism

## Comparison Summary
| Community | Price | Members | Retention Driver |
|-----------|-------|---------|-----------------|
| Hampton | $8,500/yr | 1,000+ | Peer groups + founder exclusivity |
| South Park Commons | Free (equity) | ~100–150 active at once | Fund investment + cohort bonds |
| Reforge | $1,995/yr | 50,000+ | Skills + career ROI |
| Pavilion | Undisclosed | 10,000+ | GTM career network |

## Key Insight
Elite professional communities (Hampton, SPC) achieve very high retention through small-group mechanics + high vetting. Price ($8,500) is not the barrier — the exclusivity signal and peer relationships are the value.

- SPC metrics: **Medium** (website + blog)
