# Product Hunt — User Retention & DAU/MAU

## Platform Scale
- Founded 2013; acquired by AngelList 2016 (Wellfound parent)
- Free platform; maker + voter community for product launches
- No subscription / paywall model

## Known Metrics
- **No official DAU/MAU published**
- Monthly visitors: estimated 4–5M (SimilarWeb 2024)
- Daily launches: 50–100 products/day
- Top products: 1,000–3,000 upvotes on launch day (top 5 daily)

## Retention Patterns
- Spike model: Product creators get one-time traffic boost; rarely return after launch
- Voter community: small hardcore (~50–100K regular voters) drives most upvotes
- Maker community: single launch → check results → occasional re-engagement
- "Product Hunt effect" as launch channel: 2,000 users in 3 weeks from 2 upvotes (documented case)

## Revenue / Business Model
- AngelList/Wellfound parent generates revenue from recruiting, not PH directly
- PH launched Ship (waitlist tool, $79/month) with mixed success — now sunset
- Paid Ads / sponsored placements for brands

## Engagement Benchmarks (from community posts)
- 285 clients from 48-hour PH launch: one documented case
- Average top-10 launch: 500–2,000 upvotes; 5–20K web visitors day-of
- Retention of PH-acquired users: typically low (1–5% retention at 30 days for most products)

## Key Insight
Product Hunt is a **launch channel** not a retention platform. DAU/MAU stickiness is low for most users. Valuable as a one-time acquisition spike, not ongoing community.

## Evidence Quality
- Traffic estimates: **Low** (third-party tools)
- Launch impact: **Medium** (documented founder reports)
- Retention rates: **Low** (anecdotal, no platform disclosure)
