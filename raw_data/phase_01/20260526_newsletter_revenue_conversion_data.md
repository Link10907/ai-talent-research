# Newsletter Revenue & Conversion Rate Data

**TL;DR (150-250 words):** The newsletter monetization reality is more nuanced than platform marketing suggests. Substack advertised 5-10% free-to-paid conversion as "normal" — but analysis of real publication data shows the median is 3%, and the typical creator earns around $4,000/year. The 50% annual churn rate is the most underreported metric in the industry: it means every creator must run a perpetual acquisition treadmill just to hold revenue flat. The headline success stories (Lenny Rachitsky at $2M+/year; Katelyn Bourgoin's Why We Buy at $1M/year; Matthew Yglesias's Slow Boring at $1.4M/year) are genuine but represent the top 0.05% of creators. For a founder trying to reach their first 1,000 users via newsletter mentions: these top creators represent massive potential amplification but are difficult to reach and won't cover unknown products. More actionable signal: mid-tier newsletters (10K-100K subscribers) are sponsorable, often have sponsorship pricing in the $500-$5,000/issue range, and have audiences that align well with professional-tool products. The overall ecosystem generates real money ($450M in writer GMV in 2025), but it's highly concentrated at the top.

### The 50% Churn Math

Annual churn rate for paid newsletters: **~50%**

Example calculation (creator with 600 paid subscribers @ $50/year = $30,000 gross):
- Monthly loss: ~25 subscribers
- Monthly revenue loss: ~$1,250
- To break even, need: 357 new free subscribers/month (at 7% conversion rate)
- To actually grow: even more

**Real implication:** A creator's sustainable business requires perpetual subscriber acquisition. "Newsletter businesses are treadmills, not flywheels."

Source: mazkara.studio/en/blog/substack-earnings-reality-2026/, sacra.com/research/substack/

## Sponsorship Market Data

**Typical CPM rates for newsletter sponsorships (2024):**
- Niche B2B newsletters: $30-$80 CPM
- Large general newsletters: $20-$45 CPM
- Marketing/creator economy niche: $50-$100 CPM

**Specific examples:**
- Why We Buy: ~$25,000/month in sponsorships (2024) at ~63K subscribers
- TLDR newsletter: 8-figure annual revenue entirely from advertising, 7M+ subscribers network
- Stacked Marketer: Average open rate 50%; sponsorship-funded model

Source: growthinreverse.com/katelyn-bourgoin/, newsletterbear.com TLDR analysis

## Financial Models: What It Takes to Reach $100K/Year

Model 1: Paid subscriptions only ($100/year price, 5% conversion)
- Need: 20,000 free subscribers minimum

Model 2: Lower price + ads ($50/year subs + $50 CPM ads, 7.5% conversion)
- Need: ~27,000 free subscribers

Model 3: Hybrid (paid subs + sponsorships at $100/year + $50 CPM)
- Need: ~14,000 free subscribers
- Most accessible path for mid-tier creators

Source: simonowens.substack.com/p/whats-a-realistic-conversion-rate
