# Marketing Domain: Top Newsletter Data

**TL;DR (150-250 words):** Marketing is the richest domain for professional newsletters — the highest subscriber counts, the most documented creator income, and the clearest professional audience segmentation. The ecosystem stratifies into three tiers: mega-newsletters with 1M+ subscribers (Lenny's Newsletter at 1.1M is the dominant example covering product/growth/marketing; Morning Brew at 4.2M is adjacent); mid-tier newsletters with 50K-200K subscribers that target working marketers (Demand Curve at 95K, Why We Buy at 63K, Justin Welsh at 200K, Growth Unhinged at 65K); and micro-niche newsletters under 50K that go deep on specific channels (Meta ads, SEO, B2B SaaS). The financial models vary: Lenny monetizes via paid subscriptions ($2M+/year); Justin Welsh via course sales ($4.15M total business in 2024, mostly courses not newsletter subs); Why We Buy via sponsorships ($1M/year from 63K subs); Morning Brew via advertising ($75M empire, free newsletter). Key finding: the most valuable newsletters for a B2B product audience are mid-tier ones like Demand Curve, Growth Unhinged, and Why We Buy — their audiences are exactly the growth-minded, tool-buying professionals a new product would want to reach. Sponsoring a single issue at these publications costs $2,000-$10,000 but reaches highly targeted buyers.

## Tier 1: Mega Marketing Newsletters (1M+ subscribers)

### Lenny's Newsletter (Lenny Rachitsky)
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Subscribers:** 1.1M+ (crossed 1M in April 2025)
- **Free vs. Paid:** ~1.1M free; ~18,000+ paid (4-5% conversion)
- **Revenue:** $2M+/year from paid subscriptions; $500K+/year podcast
- **Price:** ~$150/year annual (standard tier); $200-$350 bundle tier
- **Topics:** Product strategy, growth, career development for PMs and founders
- **Frequency:** Weekly newsletter + podcast
- **Started:** August 2019 (Substack); paid tier launched April 2020
- **Milestone:** Recommended by 5,000+ other newsletters on Substack
- **Creator background:** Former product lead at Airbnb
- **Growth driver:** Word of mouth ("biggest lever" per Lenny himself); guest posts in top 10; podcast cross-promotion

Source: growthinreverse.com/lennys-paid-newsletter/, growthinreverse.substack.com/p/the-5-biggest-takeaways-from-lennys, paved.com world's biggest newsletters

### Morning Brew (Business; Marketing Brew is vertical)
- **Platform:** Morning Brew Inc. (own infrastructure)
- **Total subscribers:** 4.2M+ (Morning Brew flagship)
- **Marketing Brew subscribers:** 4M+ (launched 2019 as vertical)
- **Revenue:** $75M company revenue (Insider Inc. acquisition price); professional brands generate $25M (2024)
- **Model:** Entirely free, advertising-supported
- **Topics:** Business news (flagship); marketing industry news (Marketing Brew)
- **Frequency:** Daily (weekdays)
- **Key event:** Acquired by Insider Inc. for $75M (Business Insider parent); operated 8 professional newsletters by 2024
- **Reader profile:** Business professionals; marketers specifically for Marketing Brew

Source: paved.com/blog/the-worlds-biggest-newsletters/, marketergems.com Morning Brew case study

## Tier 2: Mid-Tier Marketing Newsletters (50K-300K)

### Justin Welsh's The Saturday Solopreneur
- **Platform:** Own (kit/email infrastructure)
- **Subscribers:** 200K+ (some sources say 250K free)
- **Revenue (2024):** $4.15M total business (courses, sponsorships, templates)
- **Model:** Free newsletter; income from courses and sponsorships
- **Topics:** Solopreneur business building, content creation, monetization
- **Frequency:** Weekly (Saturdays)
- **Key event:** Creator MBA course launched Jan 16, 2024; generated $1.6M in 6 days
- **Sponsorship status:** 2025 sponsorship slots sold out in <18 hours (92.3% repeat clients)

Source: x.com/thejustinwelsh posts, thetilt.com profile

### Demand Curve (Growth Newsletter)
- **Platform:** Own (demandcurve.com) / Newsletter infrastructure
- **Subscribers:** 95,000 founders (2024); was 62,000 in late 2022; 101K on Substack listing
- **Revenue model:** Newsletter as top-of-funnel for Demand Curve's consulting/training
- **Topics:** Growth marketing tactics, acquisition channels, growth frameworks for startups
- **Frequency:** Twice weekly
- **Audience:** Startup founders, growth marketers
- **Open rate:** ~45-50%

Source: nealsnewsletter.com/p/how-we-grew-demand-curve, karolakarlson.com list

### Why We Buy (Katelyn Bourgoin)
- **Platform:** Kit (formerly ConvertKit); sends 2x weekly
- **Subscribers:** 63,000+ (approaching 70K)
- **Revenue (2024):** $1M+
- **Revenue breakdown:** ~$25,000/month sponsorships; courses and products
- **Topics:** Consumer psychology, buyer behavior, marketing strategy
- **Frequency:** Tuesdays and Fridays
- **Growth trajectory:** 1.5x subscriber growth + 3x revenue growth in 18 months to 2024

Source: growthinreverse.com/katelyn-bourgoin/, sparkloop.app profile

### Growth Unhinged (Kyle Poyar)
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Subscribers:** 65,000
- **Topics:** Growth case studies of fastest-growing startups, unconventional growth tactics
- **Frequency:** Regular
- **Background:** Kyle Poyar is former OpenView VP of Growth

Source: karolakarlson.com marketing newsletters list

### Stacked Marketer (Emanuel Cinca)
- **Platform:** Own infrastructure (stackedmarketer.com)
- **Subscribers:** 100,000+ across Stacked Marketer + Psychology of Marketing + Tactics newsletters
- **Revenue (2024):** $517,500 (down from $715K in 2023 due to lower ad spend environment)
- **Revenue model:** Sponsorships/advertising; no paid subscription tier
- **Open rate (2024):** 50% average
- **Topics:** Digital marketing news and tactics, daily digest format
- **Emails sent (2024):** 16.4M across all newsletters

Source: creatorinpublic.com/p/from-9-000-to-97-000-subscribers, stackedmarketer.com 2024 annual report (via search result)

### Creator Science (Jay Clouse)
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Subscribers:** 60,000
- **Topics:** Audience growth, trust building, income strategies for creators
- **Frequency:** Weekly

Source: karolakarlson.com marketing newsletters list

## TLDR Newsletter (Tech-Adjacent)
- **Platform:** Own (tldr.tech)
- **Flagship subscribers:** 1.6M (TLDR Tech)
- **Total subscribers (all 9+ editions):** 7M+
- **Revenue:** 8-figure ($10M+); entirely advertising-funded
- **Revenue source:** Sponsorships from AWS, Google Cloud, Anthropic, tech companies
- **Staff:** 22 full-time, all remote
- **Topics:** Tech news, AI, startups, founders, marketing, crypto, and more verticals
- **Growth:** 50,000 new subscribers/month (main edition alone)
- **Subscriber mix:** ~50% organic, ~50% paid ads (Meta, X)

Source: growthinreverse.com/tldr/, inc.com profile, newsletterbear.com analysis
