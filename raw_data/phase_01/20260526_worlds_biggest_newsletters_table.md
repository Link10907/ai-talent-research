# World's Biggest Newsletters: Subscriber Data (2025-2026)

**TL;DR (150-250 words):** This file documents subscriber counts for the world's largest newsletters, sourced primarily from paved.com's aggregated list (a newsletter advertising marketplace with financial incentive to maintain accurate subscriber data). The top-end of the newsletter market is dominated by news and general-interest publications with millions of subscribers — these are not the creator-economy professional newsletters that dominate the "Substack discourse" but rather traditional media brands in email form. The 4M-17M range (NYT, theSkimm, Morning Brew) represents a different category from Substack creator newsletters capping around 1-3M. The most relevant benchmarks for a professional-focus product: TLDR at 7M+ total (tech audience), Lenny's Newsletter at 1.1M (product/growth professionals), ByteByteGo at 1M+ (software engineers), and Superhuman AI at 1.1M (AI-curious professionals). These represent the "upper tier" of creator-owned professional newsletters. The vast majority of professional newsletters — including RadReads (50K), Why We Buy (63K), and Femke.design (40K) — operate in what the industry calls "small but mighty" territory: subscriber counts below 100K but highly engaged, highly targeted professional audiences.
