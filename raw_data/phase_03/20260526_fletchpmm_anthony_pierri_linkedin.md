# TLDR
Profile of Anthony Pierri and FletchPMM — the clearest documented case of LinkedIn thought leadership as primary B2B consulting pipeline.

## Source
URL: https://startupmountainsummit.com/linkedin-as-a-launchpad-how-anthony-pierri-turned-thought-leadership-into-a-high-growth-consultancy/
Date: 2024
Author: Startup Mountain Summit

## Key Excerpts

**Background:**
"Anthony originally planned to become a high school English teacher and later spent 11 years as a pastor, where he led services and managed teams of over 75 volunteers."

**Business built on LinkedIn:**
"Anthony and his co-founder Robert Kaminski have grown FletchPMM almost entirely through LinkedIn thought leadership, posting 3–4 times weekly with actionable frameworks, opinions, and insights."

"95% of their clients come inbound through LinkedIn, reaching 11–12 million people annually, without spending a dime on ads."

**Scale:**
"Built his product marketing agency to $1.3M in annual revenue almost entirely through authentic thought leadership on LinkedIn."

"Has helped over 200 founders rewrite their homepages with stronger positioning, messaging, and copy."

**Content strategy:**
Publishes "positioning teardowns and frameworks" 3–4 times/week. Content is "actionable frameworks, opinions, and insights" — not motivational or personal.

## Relevance
- Used in: people_marketing.md (Anthony Pierri), people_consulting.md

- Demonstrates that very high-volume LinkedIn posting (3–4x/week) can generate 95% inbound at $1.3M ARR
