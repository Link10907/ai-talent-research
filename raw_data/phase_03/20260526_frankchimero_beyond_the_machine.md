# TLDR
Frank Chimero's "Beyond the Machine" essay and talk (October 2025) — his most substantive public statement on AI and design. One of the most thoughtful designer-authored pieces on human-AI creative collaboration.

## Source
URL: https://frankchimero.com/blog/2025/beyond-the-machine/
Date: October 2025 (Kinference conference talk)
Author: Frank Chimero

## Key Excerpts

**On his workflow with ChatGPT:**
"He shared an outline of a section with ChatGPT and asked it to write in the style of 'Frank Chimero,' but he then bucked hard against the output, typically starting from scratch, noting that nothing sharpens articulation quite like feeling misunderstood."

**Centaur vs Minotaur model:**
"Two opposing postures: in the centaur model, the machine executes while the human leads and decides; in the minotaur model, the machine leads and decides while the human executes."

**Current activities:**
"On sabbatical walking NYC, investigating new creative tooling, and researching Brian Eno's collaborations with machines."

**Philosophy:**
Advocates for using AI as a designer "without feeling conflicted about it." Wants to stay in creative control while benefiting from AI leverage.

**On misrepresentation:**
AI outputs that "sound like you but aren't you" — the core problem of AI-generated text that captures style but misses substance.

## Relevance
