# TLDR
Kinsta's comprehensive LinkedIn statistics compilation for 2026. Key source for the 1% creator stat and monthly active user figures.

## Source
URL: https://kinsta.com/blog/linkedin-statistics/
Date: 2026 (updated regularly)
Author: Kinsta

## Key Excerpts

"Only 1% of active LinkedIn users create content, and they get 9 billion impressions a week."

"LinkedIn has over 760 million users, 310 million are active at least once a month, but only 3 million of those are posting content."

"Only about 1 percent of LinkedIn's 260 million monthly users share posts, and those 3 million or so users net the 9 billion impressions."

"60% of users on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter post content, making LinkedIn's 1% significantly lower."

"The low percentage of content creators on LinkedIn is notable; since more people are consuming content than posting content, your content can go farther."
