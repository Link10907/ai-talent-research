# TLDR
Growth In Reverse analysis of how Lenny Rachitsky grew his newsletter to 1 million subscribers.

## Source
URL: https://growthinreverse.com/lenny-1m-subscribers/
Date: ~2025 (milestone article)
Author: Growth In Reverse (Kell Luckson)

## Key Excerpts

- "Lenny's Newsletter hit a milestone of one million subscribers" (announced March 2025)
- Subscriber count now at approximately 1.1–1.2M (May 2025)
- Paid tier launched 9 months after starting on Substack (August 2019)
- ~18,000 paid subscribers (earlier data point; CNBC 2024 reported $500K+/year from podcast alone)
- Background: "Product lead at Airbnb for 7 years (left in 2019)"
- "Word of mouth was the biggest lever, driven by quality content people wanted to share."
- Couldn't "consistently publish 52 fresh product management articles each year in perpetuity" — diversified with guest posts

## Relevance
- Used in: people_marketing.md (Lenny Rachitsky profile)
- Key stat: 1M+ subscribers is 1% or less of US PM population (estimated 200K–500K), demonstrating that creator audiences far outstrip single occupational populations
