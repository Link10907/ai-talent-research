# TLDR
Emily Kramer's MKT1 Newsletter article on AI workflows and recommended tools for marketers. Highly specific tool recommendations organized by workflow category.

## Source
URL: https://newsletter.mkt1.co/p/ai-workflows-for-marketers
Date: 2024
Author: Emily Kramer, MKT1

## Key Excerpts

**Core framing:**
"Your job description has already evolved due to AI, whether you know it or not."

**Four core AI workflows for marketers:**
1. Data Analysis & Research — AI extracts insights from large datasets
2. Marketing Operations — automating processes to free up team capacity
3. Multi-Format Content Creation — video, audio, visual assets without specialist resources
4. Personalized Content at Scale — relevant content for specific audiences/channels

**Recommended tools by category:**
- Research & Analysis: Clay, Ignition
- Operations: n8n, Default
- Content Creation: Beautiful.ai, Goldcast
- Personalized Distribution: Tofu, Letterdrop, Naro

**Pain point articulated:** "Figuring out what tools to use amidst all the noise can be a full-time job."

**Gen Marketer framework:** "A Gen Marketer is a modern B2B startup marketer generalist who owns campaigns end-to-end and uses generative AI, AI tools, and AI agents to increase leverage and impact."
