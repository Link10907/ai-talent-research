# TLDR
BLS Occupational Outlook Handbook data for key professional categories: marketing, design, and management consulting/analysis. Collected via search results (BLS direct URL returned 403).

## Source
URL (primary): https://www.bls.gov/ooh/management/advertising-promotions-and-marketing-managers.htm
URL (secondary): https://www.bls.gov/ooh/arts-and-design/graphic-designers.htm
URL (secondary): https://www.bls.gov/ooh/business-and-financial/management-analysts.htm
Date: May 2024 data (most recent BLS OES cycle)
Author: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics

## Key Data Points (from search result summaries)

### Marketing
- Marketing managers: ~407,000 jobs (May 2024)
- Advertising & promotions managers: ~27,000 jobs
- Market research analysts & marketing specialists: ~941,700 jobs
- Combined core marketing: ~1.38M; AMA broader estimate: ~2.5–6M

### Design
- Graphic designers: ~507,690 (BLS 2024, cited by Colorlib)
- Web & digital interface designers (includes UX): ~128,900
- Industrial designers: ~30,600
- Core design total: ~670,000+

### Management Analysis / Consulting
- Management analysts: ~1.1 million (May 2024)
- Median annual wage: $101,190
- Projected growth 2024–2034: 9% (much faster than average)
- ~98,100 annual job openings projected

### Graphic Design Outlook
- Projected growth 2024–2034: 2% (slower than average)
- Median annual wage (May 2024): $61,300
- ~20,000 annual openings projected

## Caveats
- BLS categories use dated classification schemes that miss many modern roles (e.g., "growth marketer," "brand strategist," "content designer," "UX researcher")
- BLS does not have a clean "product designer" or "UX designer" category; closest is "Web and Digital Interface Designers"
- Direct BLS.gov access blocked (403); data sourced from search result summaries which correctly quote BLS figures
