# LinkedIn for B2B Salespeople — Positive Voice
Sources: HubSpot, theCMO, Brands at Play, multiple 2025-2026 social selling guides
Date accessed: 2026-05-25

## Headline Statistics
- 70% of B2B marketers view LinkedIn as the most effective platform for lead generation
- 80% of B2B social leads originate on LinkedIn
- Sales professionals who practice social selling are 45% more likely to meet/exceed quota
- Top-performing salespeople are 51% more likely to reach quota when using social selling
- Inbound leads from authority-based social selling close at 14.6% vs. 1.7% for outbound prospecting
- LinkedIn's Hiring Assistant AI (Oct 2024, GA Sep 2025): early adopters save 4 hours per role, review 62% fewer profiles, 69% improvement in InMail acceptance

## Critical Context
LinkedIn is essentially indispensable for B2B sales because the buyer's decision-making committee is on LinkedIn — and only LinkedIn has the verified employer/title data needed for account-based targeting. No alternative has this.

## Implication for Phase 2
For sales reps, account executives, BD, partnerships — LinkedIn is a daily work tool, not a vanity profile. Even when they hate the feed culture, they cannot replace LinkedIn Sales Navigator. Migration is structurally blocked by tooling.
