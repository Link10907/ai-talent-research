# LinkedIn Engagement Down 50%: Algorithm Insights Report 2025
URL: https://www.writtenlyhub.com/news/linkedin-engagement-down-50-algorithm-insights-report-2025
Date: February 12, 2026
Underlying study: Richard van der Blom's Algorithm Insights Report 2025 (1.8M posts analyzed)

## Key statistics
- Organic views: down 50% YoY
- Engagement: down 25%
- Follower growth: down 59%
- Company page reach: 1.6% of followers (down 15% from late 2023)
- Personal profiles get 561% more reach than company pages
- Company content now 1-2% of feed (down from 7% in 2021)
- Native vertical video <60s: +69% performance
- Text-only company-page posts: 0.28 multiplier (heavily penalized)
- A marketing director saw her post reach drop from 8,500 impressions to 340 overnight in April after the engagement-pod crackdown

## Direct quote
Daniel Roth, LinkedIn's chief editor: "LinkedIn is intentionally helping content reach more relevant audiences rather than larger audiences."

LinkedIn VP of Product: "Our goal is to make engagement pods entirely ineffective."

## 360Brew AI
A 150-billion-parameter AI model now scores content. It detects AI-generated text and de-prioritizes it. Independent creators report sudden reach collapses tied to perceived AI authorship.

## Significance
First-party quantitative evidence — not anecdotal — that LinkedIn's organic reach is structurally declining. This explains the rising volume of "LinkedIn is dead" complaints from marketers and creators in 2025-2026. But it also means LinkedIn is *fighting back* against the slop, partially addressing the very pain point creators complain about.
