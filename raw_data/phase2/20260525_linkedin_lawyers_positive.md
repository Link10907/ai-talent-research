# LinkedIn for Lawyers — Positive Voice (Counterweight Evidence)
Sources: GrowLaw, American Bar Association cited stats, HubSpot
Date accessed: 2026-05-25

## Headline Statistics
- 90% of attorneys are active on LinkedIn (ABA)
- LinkedIn is 277% more effective at lead generation than Facebook/Twitter (HubSpot)
- 150M+ U.S. professionals use the platform
- 76% of lawyers say LinkedIn is their most preferred social media (ABA 2020 study)
- Clio's 2024 Legal Trends Report: 40%+ of legal professionals rely on LinkedIn for networking
- Clio 2025: nearly 60% of clients pick lawyers based on trusted referrals

## Key Quote (GrowLaw, Dec 19 2025)
"LinkedIn is one of the most effective platforms for lawyers to build authority, expand their network, and attract high-quality clients in 2026."

"For lawyers, LinkedIn boosts brand awareness, grows email lists, and attracts quality leads. By sharing articles and building connections, firms can market directly to potential clients and partners."

## Implication for Phase 2
- The "LinkedIn is dead" narrative is dominated by creatives, marketers, and devs.
- For lawyers / B2B sales / HR / recruiters, LinkedIn is not optional — it's the de facto industry platform.
- Migration willingness in these segments is structurally near-zero because the platform IS the network.
