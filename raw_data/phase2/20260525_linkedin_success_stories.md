## Significance
These are the "silent majority" calibration data. People who got real value from LinkedIn rarely write viral takedown articles — they post low-key thanks on LinkedIn itself (where the takedown audience never sees).

The pattern is consistent:
- B2B sales / marketing professionals — LinkedIn works
- Lawyers / consultants / executives — LinkedIn works (referrals)
- HR / recruiters — LinkedIn is their job
- Job seekers passively discovered by recruiters — LinkedIn works

The "LinkedIn is broken" cohort is concentrated in:
- Designers, devs (have GitHub / Dribbble)
- Creators / writers (have Substack / X)
- Marketers fed up with their own profession's slop
- Gen Z early-career (haven't seen value yet)

This is the precision bias correction the report needs.
