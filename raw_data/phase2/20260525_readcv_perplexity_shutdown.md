# Read.cv Acquired by Perplexity / Shutdown
Source: TechCrunch, Cosmico, AIBase
Date: Jan 17 2025 announcement; shutdown May 16 2025

## Facts
- Read.cv launched 2021 as "sleek alternative to LinkedIn"
- Founder Tina Roth Eisenberg's design-focused community
- Acquired by Perplexity Jan 2025; team moves to Perplexity
- Users given until May 16 2025 to export data
- Read.cv shut down as a platform

## Official statement
"We've long admired Perplexity and believe great things happen when the world's knowledge is made more open and accessible." Founder also: "This is incredibly bittersweet for us, as the start of this new chapter will mark the end of our time with @read_cv."

## Notable user reaction (from press coverage)
"Users are increasingly treated like stepping stools for founders... users help grow a product with their network, their content, or their money, get comfortable with that product, integrate it into their lives... and then it's yanked out from under them."

## Implication
Even the most beloved LinkedIn alternative for designers (the polished, less-cringe option) could not reach scale sufficient to operate independently. The user trust cost is high — users who migrated lost their network.
