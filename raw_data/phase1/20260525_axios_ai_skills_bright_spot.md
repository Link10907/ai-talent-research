# Axios — The dimming job market's bright spot: AI skills

Source: https://www.axios.com/2025/11/09/job-market-ai-skills
Date: 2025-11-09

## Key findings
- AI skills are the singular bright spot in an otherwise softening US labor market.
- Jobs requiring AI skills growing while overall postings declining (consistent with PwC's 7.5% AI-job growth vs -11.3% overall).
- Wage premium for AI-skilled jobs is widening, not narrowing — counter to "skills get commoditized" expectation.

## Significance
- Macro confirmation that the AI-skill premium is real and durable through late 2025.
- This is the cleanest macro framing for our report's opening: trend 2 evidence is converging across multiple datasets (PwC, Lightcast, Indeed Hiring Lab, Axios).
