# Figma — Why Demand for Designers Is on the Rise

Source: https://www.figma.com/blog/why-demand-for-designers-is-on-the-rise/

## Key statistics on AI fluency for designers
- 73% of hiring managers see an increasing need for candidates proficient in AI tools.
- 79% say designing AI products is essential.
- 58% cite visual polish as a top-5 skill.
- 45%+ cite collaboration, systems thinking, product strategy.
- 56% of hiring managers report increased demand for SENIOR designers vs 25% for juniors.

## Quotes
- UK-based tech hiring manager: "We fully [prioritize] positions that combine technical capacity, strategy, and out-of-the-box thinking."
- Daniel Wert (search firm CEO): "[Don't hire unicorns] — multiple people with complementary strengths, not a single superhero."

## Significance
- Confirms that AI fluency is a hard requirement at the *senior* design level — consistent with Growth Unhinged GTM findings but in a creative domain.
- Junior design hiring is collapsing — same compression as law/finance. This widens the "you need to show you're a builder yourself" gap for emerging designers.
- Important caveat: the article does NOT discuss portfolios explicitly — design hiring still relies heavily on portfolios but Figma's piece is about *what's IN* the portfolio, not whether portfolio replaces resume.
