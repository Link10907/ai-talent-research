# Lightcast 2025 Generative AI Job Market Report

Source: https://lightcast.io/resources/blog/the-generative-ai-job-market-2025-data-insights
Also: https://lightcast.io/resources/blog/beyond-the-buzz-press-release-2025-07-23
Also: https://www.cbsnews.com/news/ai-job-postings-brookings-lightcast/

## Headline numbers
- Unique job postings requiring generative-AI skills: 55 (Jan 2021) -> ~10,000 (May 2025).
- 2024 vs 2023: GenAI-specific postings 66,000 vs 16,000.
- Generative AI Engineer postings: up 7x 2022-2024.
- Non-IT roles requiring AI skills: up 9x 2022-2024; other IT roles up 35x.
- 51% of AI postings in 2024 were outside IT/CS occupations (was 39% in 2019).
- 800% growth in generative-AI roles across non-tech industries since 2022.
- HR sector: 66% growth (leading all sectors).
- Education/Training: 200% growth in generative-AI postings.
- Finance: 40% growth from low baseline.
- Marketing/PR: 8% of postings now require AI, 50% annual growth.

## Salary premium
- 28% higher salary for AI-skilled postings ≈ $18,000/year premium.
- Based on 1.3 billion job postings.

## Top hiring companies (by GenAI postings)
Amazon, Accenture, Deloitte, Meta, KPMG, Google, PwC, Cognizant, Capital One, Apple.

## Key methodology
- "Skills Disruption Matrix": growth rate × importance × AI exposure.
- 350 AI skills divided into 9 clusters.

## CBS-cited stats (Brookings/Lightcast)
- 100% YoY increase in AI-themed postings as of mid-2025.
- 29% avg annual growth over 15 years vs 11% for general economy.
- 80,000+ postings mentioned generative AI in 2025 vs 3,780 in 2010.
- Manufacturing AI adoption: 4% (early 2023) -> 9% (mid-2025).
- Silicon Valley: 13% of all AI postings; Seattle: 7%.

## Quote
Joseph Briggs (Goldman Sachs): "AI is definitely visible in the micro labor market data, but it doesn't look like it's driving the overall labor market dynamic."
Elena Magrini (Lightcast): emphasises the range from "advanced AI-specific roles, such as AI engineers, to more general AI-related positions such as software developers."
