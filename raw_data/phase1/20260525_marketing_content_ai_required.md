# Marketing/Content — AI proficiency now baseline in JDs (2025)

Sources (compiled):
- https://www.indeed.com/hire/job-description/ai-content-writer
- https://newsletter.aroundthebonfire.com/p/ai-marketing-manager-job-description (Kevan Lee)
- https://stripe.com/jobs/listing/aeo-geo-marketing-manager/7844214

## Key findings
- 90% of content marketers already use AI in workflow (2025 data, cited in multiple AI content writer JDs).
- Stripe explicitly hiring an "AEO/GEO Marketing Manager" — Answer Engine Optimization / Generative Engine Optimization, focused on driving growth from ChatGPT and Perplexity.
- OpenAI hiring "Product Marketing Manager, ChatGPT for Work" — explicit requirement: translate complex AI concepts to business audiences.

## Standard JD language patterns observed
- "Knowledge of natural language processing, AI, and machine learning technologies"
- "Stay current on SEO, content marketing, AI search trends, evolving user behavior"
- "Use generative AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney) to scale content production"
- "Subject matter expert in applying generative AI to marketing — reducing repetitive work and increasing campaign velocity"

## Significance
- Strongest non-engineering evidence that AI tool fluency is now a *hard* JD requirement, not a nice-to-have.
- Reinforces Growth Unhinged finding: 1,438% growth in AI-skill-required GTM postings 2023→2025.
- The skill being requested isn't ML engineering — it's "use the tools well, integrate into existing workflow." This is exactly the profile our hypothesized target user has, but currently has no good way to display.
