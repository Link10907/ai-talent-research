# Handshake "Re-founding" — AI Data Pivot 2024-2025

## Primary facts
- Handshake = college careers platform founded 2014 (Michigan Tech alumni)
- Mature SaaS business serving 1,500+ universities, ~17M students
- 2024-2025: pivoted into **AI training data services** — "Handshake AI"
- **Laid off 15% of US workforce** (~100 people) to fund the pivot
- CEO **Garrett Lord** ran the new arm in "founder mode" — high-intensity, hand-picked technical leadership
- 500,000 PhDs and 3M advanced degree holders on platform = unique pool

## Revenue trajectory (extraordinary)
- $50M revenue in first 4 months of Handshake AI
- On track to exceed **$100M in first 12 months**
- Combined Handshake + Handshake AI projected **$300M ARR end of 2025**
- PhD/expert labor: $40-$125/hour to label and reason for frontier LLMs

## Strategic significance — three lessons
1. **The "professional network as data asset" thesis is being proven in real time**: Handshake monetized its candidate database NOT by selling to recruiters but by selling its **users' cognitive labor** to AI labs. This is a different monetization vector than the LinkedIn ad/Recruiter model.

2. **Network value is in the audience composition, not just the size**: Handshake's value isn't its 17M users in aggregate; it's the **filtered subset of PhDs/STEM-advanced** that frontier labs need for training. LinkedIn doesn't easily isolate this segment.

3. **Acquired Cleanlab (data quality startup)**: Talent grab for AI data quality engineering. Pattern: incumbent networks are using acqui-hires to skill-up for the new monetization model.

## Implication for our research
- Validates the **"data flywheel for B-end monetization" thesis** at the core of our project: a consumer-facing professional product where user value is networking/identity, B-end value is data licensing
- BUT: shows a specific viable monetization is **AI training data, not just recruiter sourcing**
- The PhD/expert vertical is being captured by Handshake; AI data labeling for non-academic professionals (lawyers, designers, marketers using AI) is still wide open
- Lesson: **niche depth + verified credentials = pricing power for AI labs**, far more than generic LinkedIn-like breadth

## Quotes
- Garrett Lord (CEO, Lenny's Newsletter interview): describes the pivot as "founder mode" — CEO personally recruiting technical leadership, enforcing high-intensity culture
- Industry coverage repeatedly cites Handshake AI as **the most successful "re-founding" of an existing SaaS company in 2025**

## Counterpoint / risks
- Heavily dependent on a small number of AI lab customers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Meta, Google) — concentration risk
- Compensation upside captures expert users, but creates two-class system among Handshake's normal users
- Could damage core student-recruiter trust if students perceive themselves as "training data labor"
