# Emerging Competitors Scan — 2024-2026

## Headline finding: **NO breakout new LinkedIn replacement has emerged**

Listicles dominate the "LinkedIn alternative" search space. The actually-cited alternatives in 2024-2026 are almost entirely the same companies as 2021-2022 (AngelList/Wellfound, Slack, Discord, Reddit, Eventbrite, Meetup, Shapr) plus the same set of personal-website builders (Bento — now dying, Carrd, Notion, Super) plus newer AI portfolio generators that are TOOLS not NETWORKS.

## What's actually happening in 2024-2026

### Pattern 1 — AI portfolio/resume generators (TOOLS, not networks)
- **Taskade Genesis**: Generates full portfolio from prompt, integrates with GitHub/Dribbble/Figma. 3,000 AI credits free tier.
- **Framer**: June 17, 2025 launched "Type a sentence, get a portfolio" — 2,290 upvotes on Product Hunt
- **Rezi**: 4.3M+ users, "Forbes' top pick" AI resume builder
- **Durable / Mixo / Resume2Portfolio**: One-click resume-to-portfolio websites
- All of these are **point tools**, not networks. They solve "make a beautiful page" but not "be discovered" or "build identity over time"

### Pattern 2 — Recruiter-side AI middleware
- **SeekOut**: 600-750M candidate profiles (LinkedIn + GitHub + papers + patents); agentic AI sourcing
- **Eightfold**: 1B+ talent profile global dataset; **facing 2025 class-action lawsuit** alleging unauthorized use of LinkedIn + applicant data
- **Findem**: 1.6T public data points from 100K+ sources (LinkedIn, GitHub, ResearchGate)
- **Common thread**: All depend on LinkedIn as the spine, scrape adjacent data sources. None build a new C-end identity product.

### Pattern 3 — Vertical pro networks (mostly already mature)
- **Doximity**: doctors, $570M FY2025 revenue (separate raw_data file)
- **RepVue**: sales professionals, 60K ratings of 11K orgs, $6M total raised. Niche success, not scaling.
- **Cord**: UK tech jobs B2B tool (separate raw_data file)
- **A.Team**: 11,000 invite-only product builders; $60M Series A; **gated, not mass-market**; launched "Team Formation AI" in 2024-2025

### Pattern 4 — Identity-adjacent attempts that resemble Polywork/Read.cv
- **Lunchclub**: AI-powered 1:1 video introductions; ARR low-to-mid tens of millions in 2025; subscription model ($20-$30/mo); ~5M matches confirmed by 2024. Lunchclub Plus = 60% of revenue. Still primarily a matchmaking utility, not an identity product.
- **STEALTH Ventures (mentioned)**: One portfolio company is building a "visual resume / techcard" — but not at scale yet

### Pattern 5 — The Profile.com hint
- **Peter Johnston (ex-Polywork) is now co-founder of Profile.com** — San Francisco-based, AI startup, but specific product unclear (stealth-ish)
- This signals the **founder of the best-known failed LinkedIn challenger** sees enough opportunity to try again. Worth tracking.

### Pattern 6 — Link-in-bio consolidation (DEAD SIGNAL for "personal page as identity")
- **Bento.me shutting down Feb 13, 2026** — acquired by Linktree Aug 2023, sunset after ~2.5 years
- Linktree also acquired and killed Koji (Jan 2024)
- **Personal-page-as-identity has consolidated into Linktree** — proves this category is "tool not network"; once the tool is commoditized, only the leader survives

## Why no breakout new player?

Hypothesis based on the survey:
1. **VC appetite for new "LinkedIn replacements" died** after Polywork/Read.cv visibly burned ~$60M combined with nothing to show
2. **AI sucked all the founding energy 2023-2025** — top talent went into AI tooling, not consumer identity products
3. **Network effects + LinkedIn AI lock-in tightened** during the same window — making the cliff steeper than ever
4. **Career-side product market is at a structural low** — Glassdoor/Indeed layoffs, Monster bankruptcy = even the legacy players are shrinking

## What's NOT being attempted (the blind spots)
- **Cross-platform aggregation of work artifacts** for non-developers (not just AI portfolio gen, but auto-syncing identity)
- **AI-applied-to-traditional-professional verification** (verified "this lawyer actually built this Claude workflow")
- **Privacy-preserving B-end data flywheel** with explicit user consent + benefit
- **Community-mediated reputation** for AI-augmented professionals (different from RepVue's anonymous ratings)
