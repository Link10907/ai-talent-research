# AI Operators Substack (Evan Lee) — Chief of Staff & BizOps jobs in AI

URL: https://aioperators.substack.com/ (multiple volumes)
Recurring publication: bi-weekly job roundup

## Volume/scale signal
- Vol 005 (early): 24 jobs
- Vol 015 (~6mo): 30 jobs
- Vol 025/026 (1yr anniversary): 33→50 jobs
- → roughly **2x increase in CoS/BizOps openings at AI startups over 1 year**
- Companies featured: Anthropic, OpenAI, EliseAI, Auctor, Arphie, Rilla, Thesis,
  Scale AI dental-vertical companies, legal AI co's, construction robotics co's
- Verticalization: roles are domain-specific (healthcare / legal / finance / construction)
  rather than generic

## What's missing
The substack lists titles + companies but not detailed JD requirements →
**no public "spec" for what AI fluency means for these hires**.
This itself is the gap — the field of AI-CoS hiring has no canonical job description,
making it harder for candidates to know what to prove.
