# Hospital-Side AI Adoption & Vendor Landscape (2025)

## Adoption rates (IntuitionLabs synthesis of ASTP/ONC data)

- 71% of non-federal acute-care hospitals use predictive AI integrated with EHRs (2024, from 66% in 2023)
- Physician adoption inside hospitals: 66% by 2024, +78% YoY from 38%

## By use case
- Clinical risk prediction: ~65%
- Billing/coding automation: ~80% (up 25 pts from 2023)
- Staff scheduling: +16 pts growth
- Documentation: ambient AI scribes are the fastest-growing enterprise category

## Hospital size disparities (2024)
- Large (>400 beds): 96%
- Medium (100-399): 80%
- Small (<100): 59%
- Critical Access: 50%
- System-affiliated: 86% vs Independent: 37%
- Urban: 81% vs Rural: 56%

## Model sourcing
- EHR vendor-supplied: 80%
- Third-party: 52%
- Self-developed: 50%

## Top vendors
- EHR: Epic (90%), Cerner/Oracle Health, Allscripts
- Imaging: Aidoc
- Capacity: LeanTaaS, Qventus
- Documentation: Nuance/Microsoft DAX, Abridge, Suki, Nabla, Augmedix

## Ambient AI scribe enterprise wins (2024-2025)
- Abridge: 100+ health systems including Johns Hopkins (6,700 clinicians, 6 hospitals, 40 patient-care centers), Mayo Clinic (~2,000 clinicians), Memorial Sloan Kettering, Inova, Endeavor Health, Akron Children's, Oak Street Health. $250M Series D at ViVE 2025. #1 in KLAS 2025 for ambient scribes.
- Suki: General availability across athenahealth's network May 2025. $70M Series D 2024.
- Microsoft Nuance DAX: Microsoft's enterprise franchise.
- Nabla: European-led, growing in US.

## Real-world performance examples
- Cleveland Clinic Bayesian sepsis AI: 10x reduction in false positives, 46% more detected cases, 6-7 hours earlier alerts
- Atrium Health AI scribes: 47% of doctors reduced after-hours EHR time, 44% less doc frustration
- Mass General Brigham oncology LLM: >90% sensitivity/specificity
- Kaiser Permanente NorCal: ~15,700 saved physician hours/year via ambient AI
- UW Health randomized trial: 30 min/day per provider reduction in charting time

## Critical gaps
- Only ~50% of hospitals do comprehensive post-implementation monitoring
- Alert fatigue, workflow integration issues
- No demonstrated financial returns from AI scribes despite burnout improvements
- Geographic variation stark: some states near-zero adoption in 2022
