# EdTechJobs.io — How AI Is Reshaping EdTech Job Market
Source: https://edtechjobs.io/blog/how-ai-is-reshaping-the-edtech-job-market
Date: 2025

## Top-line
- 21% of new EdTech job listings (July 2025) mention AI explicitly in title/tags/description
- Sectoral breakdown:
  - Corporate L&D: 31% of new listings mention AI
  - Higher Ed: 24%
  - K-12: 17%

## Hot AI roles in edtech
1. AI/ML Engineer (adaptive learning algorithms, AI teaching assistants)
2. AI Product Manager
3. AI Curriculum & Training Specialist
4. AI Ethics & Policy Analyst
5. AI Search Optimization Specialist (content discoverability)

## Specific JD examples cited
- Principal Engineer, AI/ML — Quizlet
- AI Product Manager — Noodle
- AI Professional Development Fellow — Common Sense Media
- AI Risk Assessment Fellow — Common Sense Media
- Senior AI Search Optimization Specialist — Lucid Software
- Prompt Engineer II — ACT (assessment org; requires 3-5 yrs prompt eng, Python, NLP, RAG)

## Required skills
- AI literacy / familiarity with GenAI tools
- Data analysis (SQL, Python, Tableau)
- Ethical implications + bias assessment
- Domain expertise + AI application knowledge
- "Adaptability & continuous learning" mindset

## Implication
For C-side product targeting education: the most "AI-natively" hiring sub-segment is CORPORATE L&D (31%), not K-12. K-12 hiring still trails. The opportunity is greater outside the classroom-teacher segment.
