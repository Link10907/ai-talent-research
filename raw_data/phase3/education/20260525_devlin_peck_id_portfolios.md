# Devlin Peck Portfolio Showcase
Source: https://www.devlinpeck.com/showcase
Date: 2025

## What it is
Curated gallery of instructional design portfolios from people who completed Devlin Peck's ID Bootcamp/courses. Closest analog in this industry to an "identity/portfolio showcase platform" — and revealing how narrow that niche currently is.

## Scale
~40+ featured profiles. Categories: "Recently Hired," "On the Market," "Freelancers."

## AI focus
NOT AI-focused. Showcases traditional ID work (eLearning modules, scenario-based design, articulate storyline). AI work is incidental in some portfolios, not the central frame.

## Format
One-way curated gallery, not interactive community. Functions like a teacher of a paid course showcasing his students' outcomes.

## Implication for our product
- The closest "career identity" platform in this industry is an individual creator's funnel, NOT a network.
- No equivalent of GitHub for instructional designers exists.
- A real AI-era ID portfolio platform is a vacuum — but TAM is small (maybe 50-200K English-speaking IDs globally; 2K active LinkedIn ID openings per PayScale).
- The Devlin Peck audience is also notable for being recruitable: they pay for ID Bootcamp ($1-3K+), suggesting WTP exists in this segment.
