# Josh Bersin — How AI Transforms $400B of Corporate Learning
Source: https://joshbersin.com/2026/02/new-research-how-ai-transforms-400-billion-of-corporate-learning/

## Market
- $400B global corporate L&D market
- Includes training, content libraries, L&D tech, trainers, consulting

## Paradigm shift
Traditional training -> "Dynamic Enablement"
AI-native systems generate content on-demand, employees query expert systems conversationally,
similar to "60% of 900M weekly ChatGPT users are learning something" — a participation level
traditional course catalogs never achieved.

## Learning maturity model (4 levels)
1. Static training (compliance courses)
2. Scaled learning (multi-format)
3. Integrated development (role-based pathways, hard to maintain)
4. Dynamic enablement (AI-native, replaces LMS/LXP)

## Business outcomes at Level 4
- 10x more likely to lead in innovation
- 6x more likely to exceed financial targets
- 16x better adaptability

## L&D professional role evolution
- From content curator -> capability strategist
- Decentralized governance, local business unit ownership of specialized content
- Corporate L&D focuses on strategy, leadership, compliance

## Implication
L&D professionals (instructional designers, training managers, learning architects) are being
forced into AI-native skill set. This group is much more career-mobile and higher-earning than
K-12 teachers, lives on LinkedIn, has explicit incentives to showcase AI-native skills.
