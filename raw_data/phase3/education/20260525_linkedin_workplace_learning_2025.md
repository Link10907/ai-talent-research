# LinkedIn Workplace Learning Report 2025 — L&D AI Adoption
Source: https://learning.linkedin.com/resources/workplace-learning-report (synthesis from multiple summaries)
Date: 2025

## Headline stats on AI in L&D
- 71% of L&D professionals are already exploring, experimenting with, or integrating AI into their work
- Only 36% of L&D respondents qualify as "career development champions"
- Champions are 42% more likely to be GenAI frontrunners than non-champions (51% vs 36%)
- Career progression is the #1 motivator for employees to learn
- Nearly half of L&D leaders say executives worry employees lack required skills

## What L&D pros use AI for
- Faster content creation
- Personalizing learning experiences
- Skills-needs analysis at workforce level
- Career-path mapping

## Implication
- L&D is one of the highest-AI-adoption white-collar professions (71% > 60% K-12 teachers > 53% Frontiers university faculty)
- The cohort that uses AI well is correlated with strategic career programs — they will be receptive to a "professional identity" product oriented around AI competency display
- LinkedIn itself owns this data and segment, which is a competitive risk
