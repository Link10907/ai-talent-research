# Instructional Designer salaries 2025
Source: payscale, salary.com, glassdoor, robert half, indeed, LinkedIn

## Salary
- US average: $79,513 (one source) — $92,736 (Glassdoor)
- Robert Half range: $76,750 - $122,750
- Associate-level: $66,425 average
- Entry (<1 yr): $61,589
- 1-4 yrs: $68,246
- Senior + AI specialty: pushing $130K+ in BigTech / consulting

## Demand
- 2,000+ active openings on LinkedIn (US, instructional designer query)
- BLS expects faster-than-average growth this decade
- Pandemic-era spike in online learning + post-2023 AI surge driving demand

## Profile
- Almost entirely college-educated (often Master's in Ed/InstrDesign)
- Heavy LinkedIn presence; many in EdTech / corporate L&D
- Active on Substack (Dr Phil Hardman 29K subs), Twitter/X edtech
- Career identity oscillates between "educator" and "design professional" — closer to UX in 2025
