# Continu — AI Learning Agents in Corporate L&D
Source: https://www.continu.com/research/ai-learning-agents-in-corporate-l-d

## Adoption drivers
- 40% of worker productivity lost to context switching (driver for embedded learning)
- Aim: 80-90% completion rates vs 15-20% traditional e-learning
- ROI claims: 300-500% in year 1

## Two strategic camps
1. Content generation focused: faster production, BUT "no correlation with improved retention/perf"
2. Agentic learning focused: knowledge access, 70-80% retention after 30 days (2x traditional)

## Vendor landscape (5 major)
- Eddy (Continu): Slack/Teams/SMS integration
- Harmony (Docebo): LMS-embedded
- Kiki (Thrive Learning)
- AI Companion (360Learning)
- CYPHER Agent: multilingual content creation

## Outcomes companies report
- 50-80% reduction in time finding information
- 30-60% fewer help-desk tickets
- 60-75% faster time-to-competency (8-12 wks -> 2-3 wks)
- $1.2K-$3K annual productivity gains per employee

## L&D pro skill implications
- Knowledge architecture
- Change management
- Analytics interpretation
- Strategic technology evaluation
