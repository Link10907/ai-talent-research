# Corporate training market — size and AI shift
Sources: Training Magazine 2025 report; Bersin; Technavio

## US training expenditures
- $102.8B in 2025 (+4.9% YoY)
- Large companies: average $11.7M budget
- Mid: $1.6M; Small: $333K
- Per learner: $874/yr (up from $774)

## Global
- Global corp training market: ~$395-400B (2022); 9.9% CAGR projected to 2030
- Technavio: $43.86B incremental growth 2025-2029 (7.8% CAGR)

## AI penetration
- 37% of organizations using AI for training (up from 25% in 2024)
- 75% expect to increase AI spend next fiscal year
- 25% have earmarked AI training as 2026 funding priority
- Spending on outside products/services: +29% to $16B
- Employees who get 5+ hrs of formal AI training: 12pp more likely to become regular users

## Implication
Corporate L&D budgets are huge and ABSORBING AI spend faster than K-12 budgets. L&D professionals
have economic leverage that classroom teachers don't.
