# Gallup-Walton Teacher AI Survey 2025
Source: https://news.gallup.com/poll/691967/three-teachers-weekly-saving-six-weeks-year.aspx
Fielded: Mar-Apr 2025

## Headline stats
- 60% of K-12 teachers use AI for work
- 30% use AI weekly (the "regular user" segment)
- 28% use it monthly or less

## Time savings ("AI dividend")
- Weekly users save 5.9 hours/week on average
- = ~6 weeks of time annually (37.4-week school year)
- Monthly users save less but still meaningful

## Top use cases
- Preparing to teach: 37% (monthly+)
- Creating worksheets/activities: 33%
- Modifying materials for student needs: 28%

## Quality reports
- 57-74% report quality improvements (low: grading/feedback at 57%; high: admin work at 74%)
- <16% report decreased quality in any category

## Key framing
"Educators must invest in using AI tools to earn a dividend" — explicit upskilling required.
