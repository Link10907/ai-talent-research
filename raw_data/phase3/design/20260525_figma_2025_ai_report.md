# Figma 2025 AI Report — Key findings

Source: https://www.figma.com/blog/figma-2025-ai-report-perspectives/
Sample: 2,500 Figma users
Date: 2025

## Headline stats
- 1 in 3 Figma users are launching AI-powered products in 2025 (50% YoY growth)
- 85% of designers and developers say learning to work with AI is essential to future success
- 78% agree AI "significantly enhances the efficiency of my work"
- Only 32% say they can "rely on the output of AI in their work"
- Only 20% of designers/developers report majority of projects are AI-powered

## Designer vs developer gap
- Developer satisfaction with AI tools: 82%
- Designer satisfaction with AI tools: 69%
- Developers say AI improves quality of work: 68%
- Designers say AI improves quality of work: 54%
- Developers using AI for core dev tasks (code gen): 59%
- Designers using AI in core design work (asset gen): 31%

## Design-phase use cases
- 33% use AI to generate design assets (images/copy)
- 22% use AI to create first drafts of interfaces/websites
- 21% use AI to explore different layouts/themes

## Agentic AI
- 51% of AI builders now work on agents (up from 21%)
- 61% of employees at 1-10 person companies say AI is very/critically important to market share

## Quote
"We are often thinking about how much we explain about what is happening behind the scenes, and how we design for AI assisted actions with a human in the loop."

## Interpretation
Big gap: efficiency yes, trust/reliance no. Designers lag developers in core-task AI integration — assets and code are different problems; visual judgment/brand alignment is harder for AI to nail than syntactically valid code.
