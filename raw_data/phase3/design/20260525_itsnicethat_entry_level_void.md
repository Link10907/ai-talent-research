# It's Nice That — "Welcome to the entry-level void"

Source: https://www.itsnicethat.com/features/welcome-to-the-entry-level-void-light-and-shade-digital-220925
Date: 2025-09-22

## Key facts
- Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei predicts AI could eliminate half of all entry-level white-collar jobs within 5 years.
- Dev Makker (Parsons grad): made 500+ cold LinkedIn connections to land first internship; estimates "single-digit percentage" of cohort has employment.

## Tasks vanishing
"Retouching, cropping, copy variants, banner production – once standard junior fare – are now done with a single prompt."

## Voices
- Kelin Carolyn Zhang (RISD): students accepting unpaid internships in "desperate market."
- Dev Makker: "I'm very aware that just having an internship is a rare and privileged position. When I see my friends that don't have jobs, it's not because their work is worse."
- Naheel Jawaid (Silicon Valley School of Design): "It's a bit of a 'dog eat dog world'-type vibe. It's really hard to get mentorship right now."

## Implication
The career ladder is being decoupled. "Audacity tax" — those with capital/network self-promote into rare roles. Sender-portfolio bottleneck: portfolios going into "empty void."

## Design product implications
Strong signal that juniors NEED a new way to differentiate beyond traditional portfolio sites. Portfolio + AI-capability story is a wedge.
