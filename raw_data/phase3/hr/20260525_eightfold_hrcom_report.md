# HR.com Future of Recruitment Technologies 2025-26 (via Eightfold)

Source: https://eightfold.ai/learn/hr-coms-future-of-recruitment-technologies-2025-26/

## Current adoption
- 14% extensive AI use
- 49% some use
- 37% none
- In 2023, 73% used none → big shift in 2 yrs.

## Leaders vs laggards
- 27% of leader orgs use AI extensively
- Only 3% of laggard orgs do
- Indicates BIMODAL adoption: a leader cohort racing ahead, long tail still on the fence.

## Use cases (% of AI-using orgs)
- Interview question generation 67%
- JD writing 65%
- Resume filtering 44%
- Candidate comms 43%

## Concerns
- Bias risk: 58%
- Depersonalization: 51%
- Legal compliance: 50%

## Safeguards
- AI guidelines: 57%
- Recruiter training: 56%

## TA stack satisfaction
- Only 43% rate their TA stack good/excellent
- Only 23% would rehire 76-100% of recent hires → trust in current hiring outputs is LOW

## Sentiment
- 53% expect AI use in recruitment to increase significantly
