# Josh Bersin — 2025 is "year of the agents" in HR (HR Executive)

Source: https://hrexecutive.com/josh-bersins-top-ai-headline-for-2025-its-all-about-the-agents-hr-tech-2025/

## Thesis
2025 = "huge transition": chatbots → agents. Agents *act* (vacation requests, travel booking, internal mobility) rather than just answer.

## Vendor namechecks
Workday, Oracle, Microsoft Copilot, Eightfold, Galileo.

## HR job impact (Bersin's framing)
- NOT widespread elimination
- Recruiter role shifts from "operational order taker" → "strategic partner asking deeper questions about hiring and internal mobility"
- 70% of TA leaders currently neglect reskilling — new opportunity

## Superworker thesis (separate Bersin work)
- "Superworker" = employee AI-empowered to deliver 10-100x productivity/creativity
- AI as "work enhancing," NOT "job replacement"
- Improvements in time/quality/productivity are 100x greater than cost savings from reducing routine work.

## Counter-narrative caveat
Bersin is HR-industry analyst → optimistic re HR survival. Actual layoffs (Recruit Holdings 1,300; Microsoft, IBM HR/recruiting roles) tell darker story for junior/sourcer rungs.
