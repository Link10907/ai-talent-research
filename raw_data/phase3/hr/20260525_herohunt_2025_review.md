# Herohunt — AI Adoption in Recruiting: 2025 Year in Review

Source: https://www.herohunt.ai/blog/ai-adoption-in-recruiting-2025-year-in-review

## Adoption
- 43% orgs use AI for HR/recruiting (up from 26%)
- 51% firms already deploy AI in hiring; expected 68% by EOY 2025
- 76% US / 36% EU
- 62% employers expect AI in MOST or ALL hiring stages by 2026

## Tool landscape (most-used)
- LinkedIn Hiring Assistant — sourcing, JD, outreach (GA Sept 2025)
- HireVue — ~20M video interviews/assessments in Q1 2024 alone
- Eightfold AI, Beamery, Phenom — talent intelligence platforms, $1B+ valuations
- Paradox's Olivia — dominates hourly hiring
- HireEZ, SeekOut — AI sourcing

## Task automation
- Resume screening: 82% AI-using firms
- Interview scheduling: 41% TA teams piloted AI scheduling
- Candidate sourcing time: -50%
- Chatbot engagement: ~40% firms (2024)
- Interview eval: 23% firms use AI to conduct/evaluate

## Recruiter displacement framing
"AI essentially scales up a recruiter's capacity. This indicates that AI isn't about cutting recruiter jobs — it's about enabling each recruiter to be far more productive."

- Companies using automation filled 64% MORE jobs and submitted 33% more candidates per recruiter without headcount expansion.

(Note: this is vendor-aligned source; real-world signal is mixed — Recruit Holdings 1,300 layoffs July 2025 contradicts pure-augmentation narrative.)
