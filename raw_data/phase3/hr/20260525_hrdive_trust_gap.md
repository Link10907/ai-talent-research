# HR Dive — The AI race has fostered better hiring decisions and mistrust

Source: https://www.hrdive.com/news/the-ai-race-has-fostered-better-hiring-decisions-and-mistrust/805994/
Cites Greenhouse / Checkr 2025 surveys.

## Hiring manager adoption
- 70% US hiring mgrs say AI makes hiring faster and better with fewer recruiter resources.
- BUT 68% say they're more involved in hiring than last year — AI hasn't reduced workload.

## Recruiter confidence
- Only 21% of recruiters are "very confident" their systems aren't rejecting qualified candidates, despite over half having delegated most screening to AI.

## AI candidate misrepresentation (mgr-detected)
- 91% have caught or suspected AI-driven candidate fakery.
- 32% encountered fake voices/backgrounds in interviews
- 32% detected AI scripts used by candidates
- 18% dealt with deepfakes

## Candidate behavior
- 49% of applicants submit multiple apps specifically to bypass automated filters
- 55% suspect AI evaluates them without disclosure

## Authenticity response
- 39% of hiring managers now conduct MORE in-person interviews to verify candidate identity — direct backlash against AI noise.

## Key Greenhouse CEO quote
"Candidates are doing whatever they can to break through the noise, while talent acquisition teams are drowning in so many applications."

The report calls for "good friction like identity verification" rather than more advanced AI systems.
