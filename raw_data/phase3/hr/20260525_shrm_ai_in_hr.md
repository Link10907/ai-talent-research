# SHRM — The Role of AI in HR Continues to Expand (2025 Talent Trends)

Source: https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/research/2025-talent-trends/ai-in-hr
Survey: 2,040 HR professionals, Feb 3-12 2025, US, all sizes/industries.

## Overall adoption
- 43% of organizations use AI in HR tasks (2025), up from 26% (2024).
- Adoption by org type: publicly traded for-profit 58% > private for-profit 45% > nonprofit 38% > state/local gov 35% > federal 19%.

## Primary use by department
- HR Tech: 42% | Recruiting: 40% | L&D: 39%

## Recruiting specifics
- 51% of orgs use AI for recruiting.
- Top recruiting uses: writing JDs 66% / screening resumes 44% / automating searches 32% / customizing job posts 31% / candidate comms 29%.

## Reported recruiting benefits
- 89% time saved/efficiency
- 36% reduced cost
- 24% improved top-candidate identification

## Workforce readiness
- 67% disagreed their org is proactive in training employees to work with AI.

## Quotes
- "three-quarters of HR professionals agree that advancements in AI will heighten the value of human judgment in the workplace over the next five years"
- "indispensable for interpreting cultural fit, assessing soft skills, and mitigating bias"
