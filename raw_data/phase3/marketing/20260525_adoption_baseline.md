# Marketing AI Adoption Baseline 2025

## Headline stats (Social Media Examiner 2025 Industry Report, n=730)
- 60% of marketers use AI tools DAILY (up from 37% in 2024) - massive jump in 1 year
- 84% increased AI usage over the past year
- Tool hierarchy:
  - ChatGPT: 90% adoption
  - Google Gemini: 51%
  - Anthropic Claude: 33%
- 90% use AI for text tasks: brainstorming (90%), drafting (89%), headlines (86%)
- Only 36% worry about AI replacing their roles
- Skill demand: 79% want automation workflow expertise, 69% want AI video creation

## CoSchedule 2025 stats (n=1005, Dec 2024 data)
- 85% use AI for content creation
- 93% report new AI features added to martech stack in 2024
- 83.82% increased productivity
- 84.86% improved content delivery speed
- 74.6% feel AI gives competitive advantage
- 25.6% say AI content outperforms manual; 64% say equal-or-better
- 84% report NO team size declines (note: contrast with Adweek)
- Top barrier: data privacy (40.44%)
- 85.84% plan to increase AI usage 2-3 years out
