# New Marketing Roles & Identity Taxonomy

## Vibe Marketer (hottest trend)
Source: Martech / Substack / various

- Definition: AI-native marketer who uses GenAI + no-code (Bolt, Lovable, Make, Zapier, Retool, Bubble, Gumloop, Vercel) to ship full campaigns solo
- Quote: "The T-shaped marketer was a skilled doer. The vibe marketer is a skilled conductor and experimenter."
- "Rather than executing every task, humans set the vibe: the taste, the cultural reference, the brand feeling, the emotional resonance, the strategic bet, while AI handles the production"
- One marketer can handle work of 5-10 specialists
- Phoenix and other YC startups hiring "Vibe Marketers" with comp up to $1M
- Ramp posted a "Vibe Marketing Manager" role
- Interest in vibe marketing surged ~7x in 2025
- March 2026 MarTech Conference launching dedicated "Vibe Marketing Lab"

## Gen Marketer (Emily Kramer / MKT1, Sept 2025)
- "Generalist who is fluent in generative AI"
- 5 characteristics:
  1. AI Orchestration Expert (power user, manages hybrid people+agent teams)
  2. Audience-Centric Strategy
  3. Campaign Production (cross-channel end-to-end)
  4. Π-shaped (depth in 2+ subfunctions: product, growth, content/brand)
  5. Adaptive Intelligence (knows when to automate vs human)
- "AI won't create more specialists. It will create more generalists."
- Predicts: many specialist roles will shrink or disappear

## AI Marketing Specialist (more traditional taxonomy)
- Tools: Jasper, HubSpot AI, Salesforce Einstein
- Skills: prompt engineering, marketing automation, ML fundamentals
- Salary premium: 20-30% over traditional counterparts
- Senior specialist: up to $250K TC
- Applied AI skills in marketing/sales: ~43% avg pay bump

## Top 1% writer thesis (Kyle Poyar / Growth Unhinged)
- "AI hasn't necessarily spelled the death of human-made content. What it's done is make the top 1% writer much more valuable."
- Anthropic, OpenAI hiring expert writers (OpenAI content strategist: $400K)
- Bifurcated market: AI commodifies mediocre; elevates top tier
- Top marketers shift from content creation to "strategic content engineering"
- Super embeds preset prompts to LLMs in website footer to "convince ChatGPT to say the right things about your product"
