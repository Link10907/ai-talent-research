# RepVue + Bravado - Sales-specific career platforms

## RepVue (data-driven)
- 249 companies in Cloud Sales Index, 49,720 ratings, 2M+ data points
- Q3 2025: quota attainment 43% (highest since Q2 2023)
- Data & AI subvertical nearing 50% quota attainment
- Reps anonymously submit: salary, OTE, quota attainment, inbound flow, culture, PMF
- Functions as "Glassdoor for sales orgs" + comp data

## Bravado (community-driven)
- "Reddit for sales" - War Room
- Tools to amplify voice, build personal brand
- Networking + job placement
- Backed by Redpoint

## Significance for this research
- Sales has DEDICATED career-data platforms beyond LinkedIn already
- Reveals what sales pros actually want to share: quota attainment, comp, company review
- Sales identity is built around: company joined + role + quota % achieved + OTE
- These platforms WORK because sales has objective metrics (quota)
- BUT: portfolio of "work products" not really a thing
- Track record proven via reference + interview + sometimes W2 / commission statements
