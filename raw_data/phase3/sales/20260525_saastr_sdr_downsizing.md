# SaaStr - The Great SDR Downsizing (April 2025)

URL: https://www.saastr.com/the-great-sdr-downsizing-36-of-b2b-companies-cut-sales-development-teams-in-2025/

## Source
- Emergence Capital "Beyond Benchmarks" report
- 560+ venture-backed B2B software companies
- April 2025

## Headcount Changes (past 12 months)
- SDR/BDR: 36% decreased, 44% same, 19% increased
- Account Executives: 25% decreased, 28% increased
- Sales Engineers: 14% decreased, 17% increased
- Professional Services: 17% decreased, 34% increased

## Insight
- "Evolution, not extinction" - smaller team handling same volume via AI
- Transactional/templated cold outbound = highest risk; AE/SE/complex deals = insulated
- AI SDR tools $500-$2,000/month replacing entry SDRs
- Mostly happens via attrition (not backfilling)

## Quote
"The traditional model of large teams making hundreds of cold calls is indeed dying."
