# SaaStr - 140% revenue with 1.25 humans + 20 AI agents

URL: https://www.saastr.com/we-hit-140-of-last-years-revenue-in-q1-with-1-25-humans-in-sales-and-20-ai-agents/

## Setup
- 5 core AI GTM agents + 20+ AI Agents total
- 1.25 humans in sales (down from 4+)
- Went "all in" after AI closed a $70K sponsorship deal alone

## What 1.25 humans do
- Negotiate complex/custom sponsorships
- Read interpersonal dynamics on difficult deals
- Multi-year partnership building
- AI agent management/calibration (30% of CAO time)

## What AI handles
1. Outbound sequencing
2. Inbound qualification (24/7)
3. Meeting scheduling
4. Lead reactivation
5. Q&A

## Results
- 2x prospects contacted
- New meetings from abandoned leads
- AI closed $1M+ in first 90 days
- AI SDRs send 11-40x volume of humans, "with better results"

"We'd still hire another elite human sales exec tomorrow — one who loves working with AI agents, not one who resents them."
