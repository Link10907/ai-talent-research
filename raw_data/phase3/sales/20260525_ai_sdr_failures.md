## The Receipts on Full-Autonomous AI SDR

### ZoomInfo / 11x case
- ZoomInfo trialed 11x for 1 month, churned in Feb 2025.
- ZoomInfo publicly stated 11x "performed significantly worse than their SDR employees" — threatened legal action over unauthorized logo use.
- 11x continued claiming ZoomInfo as a customer for ~12 months post-churn.

### 11x revenue gap
- 11x raised $74M from top-tier VCs.
- Claimed $14M ARR; actual number ~$3M (reported via TechCrunch / Bain Capital Ventures research).

### Artisan
- LinkedIn rate-limited and temporarily banned Artisan for ~2 weeks in Dec 2025-Jan 2026.
- Reported ~70-80% customer churn within first year.
- G2 reviews "collapsed."

### Trustpilot user complaints (11x.ai)
- "1 lead after 6 months."
- "0 meetings to show for it" after 6-month contract.
- Multi-user complaints about being locked into multi-month contracts.

### Why fully-autonomous AI SDR is failing
- Sender-reputation drop: 38-point median drop within 90 days of agentic-scale send volume (Smartlead, Instantly 2026 data).
- ESPs (Microsoft, Google) detect AI-template homogeneity.
- AI SDR hallucination rate: 12-18% of generated emails contain at least one factually incorrect company-specific claim.
- 74% of enterprises have rolled back at least one deployed AI agent due to governance failures.

## What's actually winning: hybrid models
- All successful AI sales deployments in 2026 include human BDRs/SDRs in the loop.
- 30-50% reduction in BDR headcount is typical when AI tools adopted effectively — NOT 100%.
- 83% of sales teams USING AI saw revenue growth vs 66% without (SaaStr).

## Implication for the product hypothesis
- "AI proved I'm valuable" is becoming a critical signal precisely BECAUSE the autonomous-AI-SDR claim is collapsing. The story "Here's how I orchestrated AI to close $X" is now defensible career capital. Pure quota attainment alone is suspect (Did YOU do it or did Alice?).
