# SaaStr: 15 Ways Sales Has Changed This Year in the Age of AI

Source: https://www.saastr.com/15-ways-sales-has-changed-this-year-in-the-age-of-ai/

## Key Quoted Statistics

- "36% of companies decreased SDR/BDR headcount in the last year — highest of any sales function." Only 19% increased SDR headcount.
- AI SDR sending 3,221 emails/month vs. human SDRs sending 75-285 emails/month (11-40x volume).
- Response rates: 5.5%-12% on AI SDR outbound.
- Inbound AI agent "closed $1,010,000 in revenue" in first 90 days, handling 45,188 sessions and qualifying 1,025 prospects.
- AI-native companies running 2:1 or 3:1 SE-to-AE ratios (vs traditional 4:1).

## Direct Strategic Quotes

- "Fewer traditional AEs (especially $50K-$250K ACV range)."
- AEs need to "develop deep technical chops (SE-level product knowledge)" and "move upmarket to complex, strategic deals."
- Struggling AEs rely on: "process execution, product knowledge regurgitation, scripted objection handling, pricing negotiation within guardrails. AI is better at all of these."
- "AI curiosity is now a firing offense to lack."

## What This Means for the Product Hypothesis

- The AE role is BIFURCATING: top-of-market complex AEs survive and need to prove ENTERPRISE+TECHNICAL skills. Low-ACV transactional AEs are being squeezed from below by SDR-AI and from above by SE-as-closer.
- Sales engineers are becoming PRIMARY deal closers in AI-native cos.
- Companies want to see CONCRETE evidence of AI-tool deployment, not buzzwords.
