# Bloomberry: 1000 GTM Engineering Jobs Analysis

Source: https://bloomberry.com/blog/i-analyzed-1000-gtm-engineering-jobs-here-is-what-i-learned/

## Growth

- GTM Engineering postings: **205% YoY** increase 2024->2025
- Average experience required: 4.11 years
- Median salary: $127,500
- Top payers: Vercel ($252K), OpenAI ($250K), LILT AI ($221.5K)

## Technical Requirements

- 38% of postings require SQL
- 38% require Python
- 43% of hiring companies use visitor identification (ZoomInfo, 6Sense, Apollo)

## Career Path Origins (most -> least common)
1. SDR/BDR progression
2. RevOps/Sales Ops transition
3. Early-stage startup generalist
4. Marketing/growth backgrounds
5. Technical engineering backgrounds (RAREST)

## Implication

GTM Engineer is the *first* sales-adjacent role where Clay/no-code-AI workflow skills are HIRING CRITERIA. Portfolios of Clay tables and n8n flows are starting to function like GitHub repos. But the population is SMALL (~5,075 profiles globally as of Nov 2025 per fullfunnel.co).
