# TechCrunch - 11x AI Customer Scandal (March 24, 2025)

URL: https://techcrunch.com/2025/03/24/a16z-and-benchmark-backed-11x-has-been-claiming-customers-it-doesnt-have/

## Key Findings
- ZoomInfo: "performed significantly worse than our SDR employees" - threatened legal
- Airtable: "very short" trial, "never used in production", denied logo use
- 70-80% customer churn rate within trial period
- ARR claims $14M but post-trial only ~$3M
- Engineer: "the products barely work"
- Issues: hallucinations, loading failures, manual verification required
- Toxic culture: 60+ hour weeks, founder public criticism in Slack
- Backed by a16z + Benchmark, was VC darling

## Market Implication
The flagship AI SDR company's hype-vs-reality gap suggests:
- AI SDR adoption is still very experimental
- High churn = product-market fit not solved
- B2B buyers in 2025 are increasingly skeptical of "AI replaces sales"
- Pricing/value mismatch
