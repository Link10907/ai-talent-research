# Affinity — 10 AI Tools for Venture Capital Firms (2025-2026)

URL: https://www.affinity.co/guides/vc-ai-tools

## The stack (the standard VC AI toolkit)
1. **Claude** — deep document analysis; pitch decks, financial models in single prompt
2. **Granola** — privacy-focused meeting notes (no bot joining)
3. **Affinity** — purpose-built VC CRM, relationship intelligence
4. **ChatGPT** — memo drafting, market analysis, term sheets
5. **Perplexity** — citation-backed competitive research
6. **Harmonic** — early-stage company discovery, 30M+ companies, hiring/founder signals
7. **Gamma** — AI deck builder for LP updates / deal reviews
8. **Tracxn** — sector landscaping, 3M companies
9. **Grata** — natural language search of 1.2B web pages
10. **Standard Metrics** — automated portfolio data + benchmarking

## Adoption
- **85%** private capital dealmakers use AI to automate daily tasks (up from 76%)
- **82%** of firms use AI specifically for deal sourcing
