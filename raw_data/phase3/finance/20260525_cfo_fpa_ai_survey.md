# CFO.com — 79% of FP&A teams use AI, mostly for ops (2025)

URL: https://www.cfo.com/news/79-of-fpa-teams-are-using-ai-but-mostly-to-enhance-operations/759924/
Source: Drivetrain survey of 258 FP&A professionals, Sept 2025

## Adoption
- **79%** of FP&A teams have adopted AI tools to some degree

## Tool usage (among AI-using teams)
- **93%** ChatGPT or similar LLM
- **22%** Microsoft 365 Copilot
- **20%** AI features inside BI tools
- **17%** AI-native finance tools (Drivetrain, etc.)

## Where AI is actually used (current state = operational, not strategic)
- Excel automation
- Report polishing
- Quick operational wins

## What FP&A teams DON'T yet use AI for
- Scenario modeling
- Influencing planning cycles
- Cross-functional decisions

## Skills gap
- **68%** spend 5 hrs/month or less on AI upskilling
- **15%** spend zero hours
- Only **4%** spend 10+ hrs

## Governance
- **28%** formal AI policy
- **39%** informal
- **26%** no policy at all

## Drivetrain summary quote
> "AI is helping teams move faster but not necessarily smarter."

## Takeaway
FP&A is broadly adopting at the surface (mostly ChatGPT as faster Excel/Word) but light on training & deep workflow integration. Lots of headroom for products that help finance pros build & demonstrate AI workflows.
