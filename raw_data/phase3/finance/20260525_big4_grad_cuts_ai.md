# Accountancy Age — Big 4's New Favourite Grad is AI (2025-06-23)

URL: https://accountancyage.com/2025/06/23/the-big-fours-new-favourite-grad-is-ai/

## Grad intake declines (UK)
- KPMG: 1,399 → 942 grads (**-29%**)
- Deloitte: **-18%**
- EY: **-11%**
- PwC: **-6%**
- Overall accountancy grad listings: **-44% YoY**

## Hiring mix shift
- AI-related roles = ~**7%** of all Big 4 postings last year
- Audit roles = under **3%** (AI now > audit hiring at Big 4)
- AI postings tripled since 2022 (when <2% mentioned AI skills)

## Firm initiatives
- Deloitte: internal "AI academy"
- KPMG: partnership w/ Microsoft (Azure, OpenAI, Copilot)
- PwC: enterprise customer of OpenAI
- EY: AI audit system pilot
- KPMG announced 10% layoff of US audit partners citing AI-driven manager redundancy

## Public trust gap
- Only **42%** of UK public trusts AI (KPMG research)
- **75%** report no formal AI training

## Drivers cited
Automation handles tasks once given to grads (research, drafting, doc summarization, compliance checks) + offshoring to India, Malaysia, Philippines.

## Implication
Accounting / Big 4 is the most disrupted subgroup — entry-level pipeline collapsing, AI hiring exceeds audit hiring. These professionals have STRONG reason to want a new way to demonstrate value beyond firm pedigree.
